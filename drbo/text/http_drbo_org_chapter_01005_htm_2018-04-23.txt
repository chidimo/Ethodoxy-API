             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The genealogy, age, and death of the Patriarchs, from Adam to Noe. The translation of Henoch.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               This is the book of the generation of Adam. In the day that God created man, he made him to the likeness of God.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               He created them male and female; and blessed them: and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               And Adam lived a hundred and thirty years, and begot a son to his own image and likeness, and called his name Seth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               And the days of Adam, after he begot Seth, were eight hundred years: and he begot sons and daughters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               And all the time that Adam lived came to nine hundred and thirty years, and he died.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Seth also lived a hundred and five years, and begot Enos.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               And Seth lived after he begot Enos, eight hundred and seven years, and begot sons and daughters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years, and he died.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               And Enos lived ninety years, and begot Cainan.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               After whose birth he lived eight hundred and fifteen years, and begot sons and daughters.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               And all the days of Enos were nine hundred and five years, and he died.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               And Cainan lived seventy years, and begot Malaleel.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               And Cainan lived after he begot Malaleel, eight hundred and forty years, and begot sons and daughters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               And all the days of Cainan were nine hundred and ten years, and he died.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               And Malaleel lived sixty-five years, and begot Jared.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               And Malaleel lived after he begot Jared, eight hundred and thirty years, and begot sons and daughters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               And all the days of Malaleel were eight hundred and ninety-five years, and he died.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               And Jared lived a hundred and sixty-two years, and begot Henoch.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               And Jared lived after he begot Henoch, eight hundred years, and begot sons and daughters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               And all the days of Jared were nine hundred and sixty-two years, and he died.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               And Henoch lived sixty-five years, and begot Mathusala.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               And Henoch walked with God: and lived after he begot Mathusala, three hundred years, and begot sons and daughters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               And all the days of Henoch were three hundred and sixty-five years.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               And he walked with God, and was seen no more: because God took him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               And Mathusala lived a hundred and eighty-seven years, and begot Lamech.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               And Mathusala lived after he begot Lamech, seven hundred and eighty-two years, and begot sons and daughters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               And all the days of Mathusala were nine hundred and sixty-nine years, and he died.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               And Lamech lived a hundred and eighty-two years, and begot a son.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               And he called his name Noe, saying: This same shall comfort us from the works and labours of our hands on the earth, which the Lord hath cursed.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               And Lamech lived after he begot Noe, five hundred and ninety-five years, and begot sons and daughters.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=5&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               And all the days of Lamech came to seven hundred and seventy-seven years, and he died. And Noe, when he was five hundred years old, begot Sem, Cham, and Japheth.
              </p>
             </td>

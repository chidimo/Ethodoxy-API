             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Eliphaz proceeds in his charge, and exhorts Job to acknowledge his sins.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Call now if there be any that will answer thee, and turn to some of the saints.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Anger indeed killeth the foolish, and envy slayeth the little one.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               I have seen a fool with a strong root, and I cursed his beauty immediately.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               His children shall be far from safety, and shall be destroyed in the gate, and there shall be none to deliver them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Whose harvest the hungry shall eat, and the armed man shall take him by violence, and the thirsty shall drink up his riches.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Nothing upon earth is done without a voice cause, and sorrow doth not spring out of the ground.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Man is born to labour and the bird to fly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Wherefore I will pray to the Lord, and address my speech to God:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Who doth great things and unsearchable and wonderful things without number:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Who giveth rain upon the face of the earth, and watereth all things with waters:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Who setteth up the humble on high, and comforteth with health those that mourn.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Who bringeth to nought the designs of the malignant, so that their hands cannot accomplish what they had begun:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Who catcheth the wise in their craftiness, and disappointeth the counsel of the wicked:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               They shall meet with darkness in the day, and grope at noonday as in the night.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               But he shall save the needy from the sword of their mouth, and the poor from the hand of the violent.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               And to the needy there shall be hope, but iniquity shall draw in her mouth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Blessed is the man whom God correcteth: refuse not therefore the chastising of the lord:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               For he woundeth, and cureth: he striketh, and his hands shall heal.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               In six troubles he shall deliver thee, and in the seventh, evil shall not touch thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               In famine he shall deliver thee from death: and in battle, from the hand of the sword.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Thou shalt he hidden from the scourge of the tongue: and thou shalt not fear calamity when it cometh.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               In destruction and famine then shalt laugh: and thou shalt not be afraid of the beasts of the earth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               But thou shalt have a covenant with the stones of the lands, and the beasts of the earth shall be at peace with thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               And thou shalt know that thy tabernacle is in peace, and visiting thy beauty thou shalt not sin.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Thou shalt know also that thy seed shall be multiplied, and thy offspring like the grass of the earth.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               Thou shalt enter into the grave in abundance, as a heap of wheat is brought in its season.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=5&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               Behold, this is even so, as we have searched out: which thou having heard, consider it thoroughly in thy mind.
              </p>
             </td>

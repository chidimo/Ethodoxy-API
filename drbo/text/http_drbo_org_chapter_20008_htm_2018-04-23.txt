             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Baldad, under pretence of defending the justice of God, accuses Job, and exhorts him to return to God.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               The Baldad the Suhite answered, and said:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               How long wilt thou speak these things, and how long shall the words of thy mouth be like a strong wind?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Doth God pervert judgment, or doth the Almighty overthrow that which is just?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Although thy children have sinned against him, and he hath left them in the hand of their iniquity:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Yet if thou wilt arise early to God, and wilt beseech the Almighty:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               If thou wilt walk clean and upright, he will presently awake onto thee, and will make the dwelling of thy justice peaceable:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Insomuch, that if thy former things were small, thy latter things would be multiplied exceedingly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               For inquire of the former generation, and search diligently into the memory of the fathers:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               (For we are but of yesterday, and are ignorant that our days upon earth are but a shadow:)
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               And they shall teach thee: they shall speak to thee, and utter words out of their hearts.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Can the rush be green without moisture? or a sedge-bush grow without water?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               When it is yet in flower, and is not plucked up with the hand, it withereth before all herbs.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Even so are the ways of all that forget God, and the hope of the hypocrite shall perish:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               His folly shall not please him, and his trust shall be like the spider's web.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               He shall lean upon his house, and it shall not stand: he shall prop it up, and it shall not rise:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               He seemeth to have moisture before the sun cometh, and at his rising his blossom shall shoot forth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               His roots shall be thick upon a heap of stones, and among the stones he shall abide.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               If one swallow him up out of his place, he shall deny him, and shall say: I know thee not.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               For this is the joy of his way, that others may spring again out of the earth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               God will not cast away the simple, nor reach out his hand to the evildoer:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Until thy mouth be filled with laughter, and thy lips with rejoicing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=8&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               They that hate thee, shall be clothed with confusion: and the dwelling of the wicked shall not stand.
              </p>
             </td>

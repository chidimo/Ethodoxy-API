             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Job's reply to Sophar. He extols God's power and wisdom.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               When Job answered, and said:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Are you then men alone, and shall wisdom die with you?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               I also have a heart as well as you: for who is ignorant of these things, which you know?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               He that is mocked by his friends as I, shall call upon God and he will hear him: for the simplicity of the just man is laughed to scorn.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               The lamp despised in the thoughts of the rich, is ready for the time appointed.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               The tabernacles of robbers abound, and they provoke God boldly; whereas it is he that hath given all into their hands:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               But ask now the beasts, and they shall teach thee: and the birds of the air, and they shall tell thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Speak to the earth, and it shall answer thee: and the fishes of the sea shall tell.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Who is ignorant that the hand of the Lord hath made all these things?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               In whose hand is the soul of every living thing, and the spirit of all flesh of man.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Doth not the ear discern words, and the palate of him that eateth, the taste?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               In the ancient is wisdom, and in length of days prudence.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               With him is wisdom and strength, he hath counsel and understanding.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               If he pull down, there is no man that can build up: if he shut up a man, there is none that can open.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               If he withhold the waters, all things shall be dried up: and if he send them out, they shall overturn the earth.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               With him is strength and wisdom: he knoweth both the deceiver, and him that is deceived.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               He bringeth counsellors to a foolish end, and judges to insensibility.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               He looseth the belt of kings, and girdeth their loins with a cord.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               He leadeth away priests without glory, and overthroweth nobles.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               He changeth the speech of the true speakers, and taketh away the doctrine of the aged.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               He poureth contempt upon princes, and relieveth them that were oppressed.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               He discovereth deep things out of darkness, and bringeth up to light the shadow of death.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               He multiplieth nations, and destroyeth them, and restoreth them again after they were overthrown.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               He changeth the heart of the princes of the people of the earth, and deceiveth them that they walk in vain where there is no way.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=12&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               They shall grope as in the dark, and not in the light, and he shall make them stagger like men that are drunk.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

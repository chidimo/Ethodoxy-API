             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Job expostulates with his friends: and appeals to the judgment of God.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Then Job answered, and said:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               I have often heard such things as these: you are all troublesome comforters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Shall windy words have no end? or is it any trouble to thee to speak?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               I also could speak like you: and would God your soul were for my soul.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               I would comfort you also with words, and would wag my head over you.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               I would strengthen you with my mouth, and would move my lips, as sparing you.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               But what shall I do? If I speak, my pain will not rest: and if I hold my peace, it will not depart from me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               But now my sorrow hath oppressed me, and all my limbs are brought to nothing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               My wrinkles bear witness against me, and a false speaker riseth up against my face, contradicting me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               He hath gathered together his fury against me, and threatening me he hath gnashed with his teeth upon me: my enemy hath beheld me with terrible eyes.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               They have opened their mouths upon me, and reproaching me they have struck me on the cheek, they are filled with my pains.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               God hath shut me up with the unjust man, and hath delivered me into the hands of the wicked.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               I that was formerly so wealthy, am all on a sudden broken to pieces: he hath taken me by my neck, he hath broken me, and hath set me up to be his mark.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               He hath compassed me round about with his lances, he hath wounded my loins, he hath not spared, and hath poured out my bowels on the earth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               He hath torn me with wound upon wound, he hath rushed in upon me like a giant.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               I have sowed sackcloth upon my skin, and have covered my flesh with ashes.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               My face is swollen with weeping, and my eyelids are dim.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               These things have I suffered without the iniquity of my hand, when I offered pure prayers to God.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               O earth, cover not thou my blood, neither let my cry find a hiding place in thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               For behold my witness is in heaven, and he that knoweth my conscience is on high.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               My friends are full of words: my eye poureth out tears to God.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               And O that a man might so be judged with God, as the son of man is judged with his companion!
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=16&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               For behold short years pass away and I am walking in a path by which I shall not return.
              </p>
             </td>
            </tr>

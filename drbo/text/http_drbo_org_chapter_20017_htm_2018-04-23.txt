             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Job's hope in God: he expects rest in death.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               My spirit shall be wasted, my days shall be shortened, and only the grave remaineth for me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               I have
               <u>
                not sinned
               </u>
               , and my eye abideth in bitterness.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Deliver me O Lord, and set me beside thee, and let any man's hand fight against me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Thou hast set their heart far from understanding, therefore they shall not be exalted.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               He promiseth a prey to his companions, and the eyes of his children shall fail.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [2]
               <u>
                "Not sinned"
               </u>
               : That is, I am not guilty of such sins as they charge me with.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               He hath made me as it were a byword of the people, and I am an example before them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               My eye is dim through indignation, and my limbs are brought as it were to nothing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               The just shall be astonished at this, and the innocent shall be raised up against the hypocrite.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               And the just man shall hold on his way, and he that hath clean hands shall be stronger and stronger.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Wherefore be you all converted, and come, and I shall not find among you any wise man.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               My days have passed away, my thoughts are dissipated, tormenting my heart.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               They have turned night into day, and after darkness I hope for light again.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               If I wait
               <u>
                hell
               </u>
               is my house, and I have made my bed in darkness.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               If I have said to rottenness: Thou art my father; to worms, my mother and my sister.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Where is now then my expectation, and who considereth my patience?
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [13]
               <u>
                "Hell"
               </u>
               : Sheol. The region of the dead.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=17&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               All that I have shall go down into the
               <u>
                deepest pit
               </u>
               : thinkest thou that there at least I shall have rest?
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [16]
               <u>
                "Deepest pit"
               </u>
               : Literally, hell.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

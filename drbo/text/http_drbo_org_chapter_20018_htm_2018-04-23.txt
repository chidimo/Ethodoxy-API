             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Baldad again reproves Job and describes the miseries of the wicked.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Then Baldad the Suhite answered, and said:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               How long will you throw out words? understand first, and so let us speak.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Why are we reputed as beasts, and counted vile before you?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Thou that destroyest thy soul in thy fury, shall the earth be forsaken for thee, and shall rocks be removed out of their place?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Shall not the light of the wicked be extinguished, and the flame of his fire not shine?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               The light shall be dark in his tabernacle, and the lamp that is over him, shall be put out.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               The step of his strength shall be straitened, and his own counsel shall cast him down headlong.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               For he hath thrust his feet into a net, and walketh in its meshes.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               The sole of his foot shall be held in a snare, and thirst shall burn against him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               A gin is hidden for him in the earth, and his trap upon the path.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Fears shall terrify him on every side, and shall entangle his feet.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Let his strength be wasted with famine, and let hunger invade his ribs.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Let it devour the beauty of his skin, let the firstborn death consume his arms.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Let his confidence be rooted out of his tabernacle, and let destruction tread upon him like a king.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Let the companions of him that is not, dwell in his tabernacle, let brimstone be sprinkled in his tent.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               Let his roots be dried up beneath, and his harvest destroyed above.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Let the memory of him perish from the earth and let not his name be renowned in the streets.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               He shall drive him out of light into darkness, and shall remove him out of the world.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               His seed shall not subsist, nor his offspring among his people, nor any remnants in his country.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               They that come after him shall be astonished at his day, and horror shall fall upon them that went before.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=18&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               These men are the tabernacles of the wicked, and this the place of him that knoweth not God.
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Domine, Deus meus. David, trusting in the justice of his cause, prayeth for God's help against his enemies.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               The psalm of David which he sung to the Lord for the words of Chusi the son of Jemini. [2 Kings 16]
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               O Lord my God, in thee have I put my trust: save me from all them that persecute me, and deliver me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Lest at any time he seize upon my soul like a lion, while there is no one to redeem me, nor to save.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               O Lord my God, if I have done this thing, if there be iniquity in my hands:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               If I have rendered to them that repaid me evils, let me deservedly fall empty before my enemies.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Let the enemy pursue my soul, and take it, and tread down my life on the earth, and bring down my glory to the dust.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Rise up, O Lord, in thy anger: and be thou exalted in the borders of my enemies. And arise, O Lord my God, in the precept which thou hast commanded:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               And a congregation of people shall surround thee. And for their sakes return thou on high.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               The Lord judgeth the people. Judge me, O Lord, according to my justice, and according to my innocence in me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               The wickedness of sinners shall be brought to nought: and thou shalt direct the just: the searcher of hearts and reins is God.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Just is my help from the Lord: who saveth the upright of heart.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               God is a just judge, strong and patient: is he angry every day?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Except you will be converted, he will brandish his sword: he hath bent his bow and made it ready.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               And in it he hath prepared the instruments of death, he hath made ready his arrows
               <u>
                for them that burn
               </u>
               .
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Behold he hath been in labour with injustice; he hath conceived sorrow, and brought forth iniquity.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [14]
               <u>
                "For them that burn"
               </u>
               : That is, against the persecutors of his saints.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               He hath opened a pit and dug it; and he is fallen into the hole he made.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               His sorrow shall be turned on his own head: and his iniquity shall comedown upon his crown.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=7&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               I will give glory to the Lord according to his justice: and will sing to the name of the Lord the most high.
              </p>

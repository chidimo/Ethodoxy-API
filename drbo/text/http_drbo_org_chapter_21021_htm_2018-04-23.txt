             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Deus Deus meus. Christ's passion: and the conversion of the Gentiles.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Unto the end, for the morning protection, a psalm for David.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               O God my God, look upon me: why hast thou forsaken me? Far from my salvation are
               <u>
                the words of my sins
               </u>
               .
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               O my God, I shall cry by day, and thou wilt not hear: and by night, and it shall not be reputed as folly in me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               But thou dwellest in the holy place, the praise of Israel.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               In thee have our fathers hoped: they have hoped, and thou hast delivered them.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [2]
               <u>
                "The words of my sins"
               </u>
               : That is, the sins of the world, which I have taken upon myself, cry out against me, and are the cause of all my sufferings.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               They cried to thee, and they were saved: they trusted in thee, and were not confounded.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               But I am a worm, and no man: the reproach of men, and the outcast of the people.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               All they that saw me have laughed me to scorn: they have spoken with the lips, and wagged the head.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               He hoped in the Lord, let him deliver him: let him save him, seeing he delighteth in him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               For thou art he that hast drawn me out of the womb: my hope from the breasts of my mother.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               I was cast upon thee from the womb. From my mother's womb thou art my God,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Depart not from me. For tribulation is very near: for there is none to help me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Many calves have surrounded me: fat bulls have besieged me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               They have opened their mouths against me, as a lion ravening and roaring.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               I am poured out like water; and all my bones are scattered. My heart is become like wax melting in the midst of my bowels.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               My strength is dried up like a potsherd, and my tongue hath cleaved to my jaws: and thou hast brought me down into the dust of death.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               For many dogs have encompassed me: the council of the malignant hath besieged me. They have dug my hands and feet.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               They have numbered all my bones. And they have looked and stared upon me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               They parted my garments amongst them; and upon my vesture they cast lots.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               But thou, O Lord, remove not thy help to a distance from me; look towards my defence.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Deliver, O God, my soul from the sword: my only one from the hand of the dog.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Save me from the lion's mouth; and my lowness from the horns of the unicorns.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               I will declare thy name to my brethren: in the midst of the church will I praise thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               Ye that fear the Lord, praise him: all ye the seed of Jacob, glorify him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Let all the seed of Israel fear him: because he hath not slighted nor despised the supplication of the poor man. Neither hath he turned away his face from me: and when I cried to him he heard me.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               With thee is my praise in a great church: I will pay my vows in the sight of them that fear him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               The poor shall eat and shall be filled: and they shall praise the Lord that seek him: their hearts shall live for ever and ever.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               All the ends of the earth shall remember, and shall be converted to the Lord: And all the kindreds of the Gentiles shall adore in his sight.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               For the kingdom is the Lord's; and he shall have dominion over the nations.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               All the fat ones of the earth have eaten and have adored: all they that go down to the earth shall fall before him.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               And to him my soul shall live: and my seed shall serve him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=21&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               There shall be declared to the Lord a generation to come: and the heavens shall shew forth his justice to a people that shall be born, which the Lord hath made.
              </p>

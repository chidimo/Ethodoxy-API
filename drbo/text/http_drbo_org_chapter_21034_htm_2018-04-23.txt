             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Judica, Domine, nocentes me. David, in the person of Christ, prayeth against his persecutors: prophetically foreshewing the punishments that shall fall upon them.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               For David himself. Judge thou, O Lord, them that wrong me: overthrow them that fight against me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Take hold of arms and shield: and rise up to help me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Bring out the sword, and shut up the way against them that persecute me: say to my soul: I am thy salvation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Let them be confounded and ashamed that seek after my soul. Let them be turned back and be confounded that devise against me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Let them become as dust before the wind: and let the angel of the Lord straiten them.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Let their way become dark and slippery; and let the angel of the Lord pursue them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               For without cause they have hidden their net for me unto destruction: without cause they have upbraided my soul.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Let the snare which he knoweth not come upon him: and let the net which he hath hidden catch him: and let the net which he hath hidden catch him: and into that very snare let them fall.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               But my soul shall rejoice in the Lord; and shall be delighted in his salvation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               All my bones shall say: Lord, who is like to thee? Who deliverest the poor from the hand of them that are stronger than he; the needy and the poor from them that strip him.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Unjust witnesses rising up have asked me things I knew not.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               They repaid me evil for good: to the depriving me of my soul.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               But as for me, when they were troublesome to me, I was clothed with haircloth. I humbled my soul with fasting; and my prayer shall be turned into my bosom.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               As a neighbour and as an own brother, so did I please: as one mourning and sorrowful so was I humbled.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               But they rejoiced against me, and came together: scourges were gathered together upon me, and I knew not.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               They were separated, and repented not: they tempted me, they scoffed at me with scorn: they gnashed upon me with their teeth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Lord, when wilt thou look upon me? rescue thou my soul from their malice: my only one from the lions.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               I will give thanks to thee in a great church; I will praise thee in a strong people.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Let not them that are my enemies wrongfully rejoice over me: who have hated me without cause, and wink with the eyes.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               For they spoke indeed peaceably to me; and speaking in the anger of the earth they devised guile.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               And they opened their mouth wide against me; they said: Well done, well done, our eyes have seen it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Thou hast seen, O Lord, be not thou silent: O Lord, depart not from me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Arise, and be attentive to my judgment: to my cause, my God, and my Lord.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               Judge me, O Lord my God according to thy justice, and let them not rejoice over me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Let them not say in their hearts: It is well, it is well, to our mind: neither let them say: We have swallowed him up.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               Let them blush: and be ashamed together, who rejoice at my evils. Let them be clothed with confusion and shame, who speak great things against me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               Let them rejoice and be glad, who are well pleased with my justice, and let them say always: The Lord be magnified, who delights in the peace of his servant.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=34&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               And my tongue shall meditate thy justice, thy praise all the day long.
              </p>

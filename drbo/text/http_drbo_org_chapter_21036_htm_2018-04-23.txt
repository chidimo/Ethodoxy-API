             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Noli aemulari. An exhortation to despise this world; and the short prosperity of the wicked; and to trust in Providence.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               A psalm for David himself. Be not emulous of evildoers; nor envy them that work iniquity.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               For they shall shortly wither away as grass, and as the green herbs shall quickly fall.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Trust in the Lord, and do good, and dwell in the land, and thou shalt be fed with its riches.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Delight in the Lord, and he will give thee the requests of thy heart.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Commit thy way to the Lord, and trust in him, and he will do it.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               And he will bring forth thy justice as the light, and thy judgment as the noonday.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Be subject to the Lord and pray to him Envy not the man who prospereth in his way; the man who doth unjust things.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Cease from anger, and leave rage; have no emulation to do evil.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               For the evildoers shall be cut off: but they that wait upon the Lord shall inherit the land.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               For yet a little while, and the wicked shall not be: and thou shalt seek his place, and shalt not find it.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               But the meek shall inherit the land, and shall delight in abundance of peace.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               The sinner shall watch the just man: and shall gnash upon him with his teeth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               But the Lord shall laugh at him: for he foreseeth that his day shall come.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               The wicked have drawn out the sword: they have bent their bow. To cast down the poor and needy, to kill the upright of heart.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Let their sword enter into their own hearts, and let their bow be broken.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               Better is a little to the just, than the great riches of the wicked.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               For the arms of the wicked shall be broken in pieces; but the Lord strengtheneth the just.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               The Lord knoweth the days of undefiled; and their inheritance shall be for ever.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               They shall not be confounded in the evil time; and in the days of famine they shall be filled:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Because the wicked shall perish. And the enemies of the Lord, presently after they shall be honoured and exalted, shall come to nothing and vanish like smoke.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               The sinner shall borrow, and not pay again; but the just sheweth mercy and shall give.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               For such as bless him shall inherit the land: but such as curse him shall perish.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               With the Lord shall the steps of a man be directed, and he shall like well his way.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               When he shall fall he shall not be bruised, for the Lord putteth his hand under him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               I have been young, and now am old; and I have not seen the just forsaken, nor his seed seeking bread.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               He sheweth mercy, and lendeth all the day long; and his seed shall be in blessing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               Decline from evil and do good, and dwell for ever and ever.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               For the Lord loveth judgment, and will not forsake his saints: they shall be preserved for ever. The unjust shall be punished, and the seed of the wicked shall perish.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               But the just shall inherit the land, and shall dwell therein for evermore.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               The mouth of the just shall meditate wisdom: and his tongue shall speak judgment.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               The law of his God is in his heart, and his steps shall not be supplanted.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               The wicked watcheth the just man, and seeketh to put him to death,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               But the Lord will not leave in his hands; nor condemn him when he shall be judged.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               Expect the Lord and keep his way: and he will exalt thee to inherit the land: when the sinners shall perish thou shalt see.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=35-#x">
                [35]
               </a>
               I have seen the wicked highly exalted, and lifted up like the cedars of Libanus.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=36-#x">
                [36]
               </a>
               And I passed by, and lo, he was not: and I sought him and his place was not found.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=37-#x">
                [37]
               </a>
               Keep innocence, and behold justice: for there are remnants for the peaceable man.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=38-#x">
                [38]
               </a>
               But the unjust shall be destroyed together: the remnants of the wicked shall perish.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=39-#x">
                [39]
               </a>
               But the salvation of the just is from the Lord, and he is their protector in the time of trouble.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=36&amp;l=40-#x">
                [40]
               </a>
               And the Lord will help them and deliver them: and he will rescue them from the wicked, and save them, because they have hoped in him.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Benedic, anima. God is to be praised for his mighty works, and wonderful providence.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               For David himself. Bless the Lord, O my soul: O Lord my God, thou art exceedingly great. Thou hast put on praise and beauty:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               And art clothed with light as with a garment. Who stretchest out the heaven like a pavilion:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Who coverest the higher rooms thereof with water. Who makest the clouds thy chariot: who walkest upon the wings of the winds.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Who makest thy angels spirits: and thy ministers a burning fire.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Who hast founded the earth upon its own bases: it shall not be moved for ever and ever.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               The deep like a garment is its clothing: above the mountains shall the waters stand.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               At thy rebuke they shall flee: at the voice of thy thunder they shall fear.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               The mountains ascend, and the plains descend into the place which thou hast founded for them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Thou hast set a bound which they shall not pass over; neither shall they return to cover the earth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Thou sendest forth springs in the vales: between the midst of the hills the waters shall pass.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               All the beasts of the field shall drink: the wild asses shall expect in their thirst.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Over them the birds of the air shall dwell: from the midst of the rocks they shall give forth their voices.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Thou waterest the hills from thy upper rooms: the earth shall be filled with the fruit of thy works:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Bringing forth grass for cattle, and herb for the service of men. That thou mayst bring bread out of the earth:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               And that wine may cheer the heart of man. That he may make the face cheerful with oil: and that bread may strengthen man's heart.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               The trees of the field shall be filled, and the cedars of Libanus which he hath planted:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               There the sparrows shall make their nests. The highest of them is the house of the heron.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               The high hills are a refuge for the harts, the rock for the irchins.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               He hath made the moon for seasons: the sun knoweth his going down.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Thou hast appointed darkness, and it is night: in it shall all the beasts of the woods go about:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               The young lions roaring after their prey, and seeking their meat from God.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               The sun ariseth, and they are gathered together: and they shall lie down in their dens.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Man shall go forth to his work, and to his labour until the evening.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               How great are thy works, O Lord? thou hast made all things in wisdom: the earth is filled with thy riches.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               So is this great sea, which stretcheth wide its arms: there are creeping things without number: Creatures little and great.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               There the ships shall go. This sea dragon which thou hast formed to play therein.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               All expect of thee that thou give them food in season.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               What thou givest to them they shall gather up: when thou openest thy hand, they shall all be filled with good.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               But if thou turnest away thy face, they shall be troubled: thou shalt take away their breath, and they shall fail, and shall return to their dust.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               Thou shalt send forth thy spirit, and they shall be created: and thou shalt renew the face of the earth.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               May the glory of the Lord endure for ever: the Lord shall rejoice in his works.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               He looketh upon the earth, and maketh it tremble: he toucheth the mountains, and they smoke.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               I will sing to the Lord as long as I live: I will sing praise to my God while I have my being.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               Let my speech be acceptable to him: but I will take delight in the Lord.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=103&amp;l=35-#x">
                [35]
               </a>
               Let sinners be consumed out of the earth, and the unjust, so that they be no more: O my soul, bless thou the Lord.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Confitemini Domino. A confession of the manifold sins and ingratitudes of the Israelites. Alleluia.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Alleluia. Give glory to the Lord, for he is good: for his mercy endureth for ever.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Who shall declare the powers of the Lord? who shall set forth all his praises?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Blessed are they that keep judgment, and do justice at all times.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Remember us, O Lord, in the favour of thy people: visit us with thy salvation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               That we may see the good of thy chosen, that we may rejoice in the joy of thy nation: that thou mayst be praised with thy inheritance.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               We have sinned with our fathers: we have acted unjustly, we have wrought iniquity.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Our fathers understood not thy wonders in Egypt: they remembered not the multitude of thy mercies: And they provoked to wrath going up to the sea, even the Red Sea.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               And he saved them for his own name's sake: that he might make his power known.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               And he rebuked the Red Sea, and it was dried up: and he led them through the depths, as in a wilderness.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               And he saved them from the hand of them that hated them: and he redeemed them from the hand of the enemy.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               And the water covered them that afflicted them: there was not one of them left.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               And they believed his words: and they sang his praises.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               They had quickly done, they forgot his works: and they waited not for his counsels.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               And they coveted their desire in the desert: and they tempted God in the place without water.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               And he gave them their request: and sent fulness into their souls.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               And they provoked Moses in the camp, Aaron the holy one of the Lord.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               The earth opened and swallowed up Dathan: and covered the congregation of Abiron.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               And a fire was kindled in their congregation: the flame burned the wicked.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               They made also a calf in Horeb: and they adored the graven thing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               And they changed their glory into the likeness of a calf that eateth grass.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               They forgot God, who saved them, who had done great things in Egypt,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Wondrous works in the land of Cham: terrible things in the Red Sea.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               And he said that he would destroy them: had not Moses his chosen stood before him in the breach: To turn away his wrath, lest he should destroy them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               And they set at nought the desirable land. They believed not his word,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               And they murmured in their tents: they hearkened not to the voice of the Lord.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               And he lifted up his hand over them: to overthrow them in the desert;
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               And to cast down their seed among the nations, and to scatter them in the countries.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               They also were
               <u>
                initiated
               </u>
               to Beelphegor: and ate the sacrifices of
               <u>
                the dead
               </u>
               .
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               And they provoked him with their inventions: and destruction was multiplied among them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               Then Phinees stood up, and pacified him: and the slaughter ceased.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [28]
               <u>
                "Initiated"
               </u>
               : That is, they dedicated, or consecrated themselves to the idol of the Moabites and Madianites, called Beelphegor, or Baal-Peor. Num. 25. 3.-- Ibid.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [28]
               <u>
                "The dead"
               </u>
               : Viz., idols without life.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               And it was reputed to him unto justice, to generation and generation for evermore.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               They provoked him also at the waters of contradiction: and Moses was afflicted for their sakes:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               Because they exasperated his spirit. And
               <u>
                he distinguished with his lips
               </u>
               .
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               They did not destroy the nations of which the Lord spoke unto them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=35-#x">
                [35]
               </a>
               And they were mingled among the heathens, and learned their works:
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [33]
               <u>
                "He distinguished with his lips"
               </u>
               : Moses, by occasion of the people's rebellion and incredulity, was guilty of distinguishing with his lips; when, instead of speaking to the rock, as God had commanded, he said to the people, with a certain hesitation in his faith, Hear ye, rebellious and incredulous: Can we from this rock bring out water for you? Num. 20. 10.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=36-#x">
                [36]
               </a>
               And served their idols, and it became a stumblingblock to them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=37-#x">
                [37]
               </a>
               And they sacrificed their sons, and their daughters to devils.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=38-#x">
                [38]
               </a>
               And they shed innocent blood: the blood of their sons and of their daughters which they sacrificed to the idols of Chanaan. And the land was polluted with blood,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=39-#x">
                [39]
               </a>
               And was defiled with their works: and they went aside after their own inventions.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=40-#x">
                [40]
               </a>
               And the Lord was exceedingly angry with his people: and he abhorred his inheritance.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=41-#x">
                [41]
               </a>
               And he delivered them into the hands of the nations: and they that hated them had dominion over them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=42-#x">
                [42]
               </a>
               And their enemies afflicted them: and they were humbled under their hands:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=43-#x">
                [43]
               </a>
               Many times did he deliver them. But they provoked him with their counsel: and they were brought low by their iniquities.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=44-#x">
                [44]
               </a>
               And he saw when they were in tribulation: and he heard their prayer.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=45-#x">
                [45]
               </a>
               And he was mindful of his covenant: and repented according to the multitude of his mercies.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=46-#x">
                [46]
               </a>
               And he gave them unto mercies, in the sight of all those that had made them captives.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=47-#x">
                [47]
               </a>
               Save us, O Lord, our God: and gather us from among nations: That we may give thanks to thy holy name, and may glory in thy praise.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=105&amp;l=48-#x">
                [48]
               </a>
               Blessed be the Lord the God of Israel, from everlasting to everlasting: and let all the people say: So be it, so be it.
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Beati immaculati. Of the excellence of virtue consisting in the love and observance of the commandments of God. Alleluia.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               ALEPH. Blessed are the undefiled in the way, who walk in the law of the Lord.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Blessed are they who search
               <u>
                his testimonies
               </u>
               : that seek him with their whole heart.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               For they that work iniquity, have not walked in his ways.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Thou hast commanded thy commandments to be kept most diligently.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               O! that my ways may be directed to keep thy justifications.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [2]
               <u>
                "His testimonies"
               </u>
               : The commandments of God are called his testimonies, because they testify his holy will unto us. Note here, that in almost every verse of this psalm (which in number are 176) the word and law of God, and the love and observance of it, is perpetually inculcated, under a variety of denominations, all signifying the same thing.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Then shall I not be confounded, when I shall look into all thy commandments.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               I will praise thee with uprightness of heart, when I shall have learned the judgments of thy justice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               I will keep thy justifications: O! do not thou utterly forsake me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               BETH. By what doth a young man correct his way? by observing thy words.

               </a>
               AIN. I have done judgment and justice: give me not up to them that slander me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=122-#x">
                [122]
               </a>
               Uphold thy servant unto good: let not the proud calumniate me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=123-#x">
                [123]
               </a>
               My eyes have fainted after thy salvation: and for the word of thy justice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=124-#x">
                [124]
               </a>
               Deal with thy servant according to thy mercy: and teach me thy justifications.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=125-#x">
                [125]
               </a>
               I am thy servant: give me understanding that I may know thy testimonies.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=126-#x">
                [126]
               </a>
               It is time, O Lord, to do: they have dissipated thy law.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=127-#x">
                [127]
               </a>
               Therefore have I loved thy commandments above gold and the topaz.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=128-#x">
                [128]
               </a>
               Therefore was I directed to all thy commandments: I have hated all wicked ways.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=129-#x">
                [129]
               </a>
               PHE. Thy testimonies are wonderful: therefore my soul hath sought them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=130-#x">
                [130]
               </a>
               The declaration of thy words giveth light: and giveth understanding to little ones.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=131-#x">
                [131]
               </a>
               I opened my mouth and panted: because I longed for thy commandments.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=132-#x">
                [132]
               </a>
               Look thou upon me, and have mercy on me, according to the judgment of them that love thy name.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=133-#x">
                [133]
               </a>
               Direct my steps according to thy word: and let no iniquity have dominion over me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=134-#x">
                [134]
               </a>
               Redeem me from the calumnies of men: that I may keep thy commandments.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=135-#x">
                [135]
               </a>
               Make thy face to shine upon thy servant: and teach me thy justifications.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=136-#x">
                [136]
               </a>
               My eyes have sent forth springs of water: because they have not kept thy law.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=137-#x">
                [137]
               </a>
               SADE. Thou art just, O Lord: and thy judgment is right.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=138-#x">
                [138]
               </a>
               Thou hast commanded justice thy testimonies: and thy truth exceedingly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=139-#x">
                [139]
               </a>
               My zeal hath made me pine away: because my enemies forgot thy words.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=140-#x">
                [140]
               </a>
               Thy word is exceedingly refined: and thy servant hath loved it.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=141-#x">
                [141]
               </a>
               I am very young and despised; but I forgot not thy justifications.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=142-#x">
                [142]
               </a>
               Thy justice is justice for ever: and thy law is the truth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=143-#x">
                [143]
               </a>
               Trouble and anguish have found me: thy commandments are my meditation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=144-#x">
                [144]
               </a>
               Thy testimonies are justice for ever: give me understanding, and I shall live.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=145-#x">
                [145]
               </a>
               COPH. I cried with my whole heart, hear me, O Lord: I will seek thy justifications.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=146-#x">
                [146]
               </a>
               I cried unto thee, save me: that I may keep thy commandments.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=147-#x">
                [147]
               </a>
               I prevented the dawning of the day, and cried: because in thy words I very much hoped.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=148-#x">
                [148]
               </a>
               My eyes to thee have prevented the morning: that I might meditate on thy words.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=149-#x">
                [149]
               </a>
               Hear thou my voice, O Lord, according to thy mercy: and quicken me according to thy mercy.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=150-#x">
                [150]
               </a>
               They that persecute me have drawn nigh to iniquity; but they are gone far off from the law.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=151-#x">
                [151]
               </a>
               Thou art near, O Lord: and all thy ways are truth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=152-#x">
                [152]
               </a>
               I have known from the beginning concerning thy testimonies: that thou hast founded them for ever.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=153-#x">
                [153]
               </a>
               RES. See my humiliation and deliver me: for I have not forgotten the law.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=154-#x">
                [154]
               </a>
               Judge my judgment and redeem me: quicken thou me for thy word's sake.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=155-#x">
                [155]
               </a>
               Salvation is far from sinners; because they have not sought thy justifications.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=156-#x">
                [156]
               </a>
               Many, O Lord, are thy mercies: quicken me according to thy judgment.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=157-#x">
                [157]
               </a>
               Many are they that persecute me, and afflict me; but I have not declined from thy testimonies.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=158-#x">
                [158]
               </a>
               I beheld the transgressors, and I pined away; because they kept not thy word.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=159-#x">
                [159]
               </a>
               Behold I have loved thy commandments, O Lord; quicken me thou in thy mercy.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=160-#x">
                [160]
               </a>
               The beginning of thy words is truth: all the judgments of thy justice are for ever.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=161-#x">
                [161]
               </a>
               SIN. Princes have persecuted me without cause: and my heart hath been in awe of thy words.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=162-#x">
                [162]
               </a>
               I will rejoice at thy words, as one that hath found great spoil.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=163-#x">
                [163]
               </a>
               I have hated and abhorred iniquity; but I have loved thy law.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=164-#x">
                [164]
               </a>
               Seven times a day I have given praise to thee, for the judgments of thy justice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=165-#x">
                [165]
               </a>
               Much peace have they that love thy law, and to them there is no stumbling block.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=166-#x">
                [166]
               </a>
               I looked to thy salvation, O Lord: and I loved thy commandments.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=167-#x">
                [167]
               </a>
               My soul hath kept thy testimonies: and hath loved them exceedingly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=168-#x">
                [168]
               </a>
               I have kept thy commandments and thy testimonies: because all my ways are in thy sight.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=169-#x">
                [169]
               </a>
               TAU. Let my supplication, O Lord, come near in thy sight: give me understanding according to thy word.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=170-#x">
                [170]
               </a>
               Let my request come in before thee; deliver thou me according to thy word.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=171-#x">
                [171]
               </a>
               My lips shall utter a hymn, when thou shalt teach me thy justifications.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=172-#x">
                [172]
               </a>
               My tongue shall pronounce thy word: because all thy commandments are justice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=173-#x">
                [173]
               </a>
               Let thy hand be with me to save me; for I have chosen thy precepts.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=174-#x">
                [174]
               </a>
               I have longed for thy salvation, O Lord; and thy law is my meditation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=175-#x">
                [175]
               </a>
               My soul shall live and shall praise thee: and thy judgments shall help me.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=118&amp;l=176-#x">
                [176]
               </a>
               I have gone astray like a sheep that is lost: seek thy servant, because I have not forgotten thy commandments.
              </p>

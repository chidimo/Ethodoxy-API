             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The advantages of wisdom: and the evils from which it delivers.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               My son, if thou wilt receive my words, and wilt hide my commandments with thee,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               That thy ear may hearken to wisdom: Incline thy heart to know prudence:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               For if thou shalt call for wisdom, and incline thy heart to prudence:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               If thou shalt seek her as money, and shalt dig for her as for a treasure:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Then shalt thou understand the fear of the Lord, and shalt find the knowledge of God.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Because the Lord giveth wisdom: and out of his mouth cometh prudence and knowledge.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               He will keep the salvation of the righteous, and protect them that walk in simplicity.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Keeping the paths of justice, and guarding the ways of saints.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Then shalt thou understand justice, and judgment, and equity, and every good path.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               If wisdom shall enter into thy heart, and knowledge please thy soul:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Counsel shall keep thee, and prudence shall preserve thee,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               That thou mayst be delivered from the evil way, and from the man that speaketh perverse things:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Who leave the right way, and walk by dark ways:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Who are glad when they have done evil, and rejoice in most wicked things:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Whose ways are perverse, and their steps infamous.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               That thou mayst be delivered from the strange women, and from the stranger, who softeneth her words:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               And forsaketh the guide of her youth,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               And hath forgotten the covenant of her God: for her house inclineth unto death, and her paths to hell.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               None that go in unto her shall return again, neither shall they take hold of the paths of life,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               That thou mayst walk in a good way: and mayst keep the paths of the just.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               For they that are upright shall dwell in the earth, and the simple shall continue in it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=2&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               But the wicked shall be destroyed from the earth: and they that do unjustly shall be taken away from it.
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               A further exhortation to seek after wisdom.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Hear, ye children, the instruction of a father, and attend that you may know prudence.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               I will give you a good gift, forsake not my law.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               For I also was my father's son, tender and as an only son in the sight of my mother:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               And he taught me, and said: Let thy heart receive my words, keep my commandments, and thou shalt live.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Get wisdom, get prudence: forget not, neither decline from the words of my mouth.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Forsake her not, and she shall keep thee: love her, and she shall preserve thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               The beginning of wisdom, get wisdom, and with all thy possession purchase prudence.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Take hold on her, and she shall exalt thee: thou shalt be glorified by her, when thou shalt embrace her.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               She shall give to thy head increase of graces, and protect thee with a noble crown.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Hear, O my son, and receive my words, that years of life may be multiplied to thee.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               I will shew thee the way of wisdom, I will lead thee by the paths of equity:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Which when thou shalt have entered, thy steps shall not be straitened, and when thou runnest thou shalt not meet a stumblingblock.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Take hold on instruction, leave it not: keep it, because it is thy life.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Be not delighted in the paths of the wicked, neither let the way of evil men please thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Flee from it, pass not by it: go aside, and forsake it.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               For they sleep not except they have done evil: and their sleep is taken away unless they have made some to fall.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               They eat the bread of wickedness, and drink the wine of iniquity.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               But the path of the just, as a shining light, goeth forwards and increaseth even to perfect day.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               The way of the wicked is darksome: they know not where they fall.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               My son, hearken to my words, and incline thy ear to my sayings.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Let them not depart from thy eyes, keep them in the midst of thy heart:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               For they are life to those that find them, and health to all flesh.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               With all watchfulness keep thy heart, because life issueth out from it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               Remove from thee a froward mouth, and let detracting lips be far from thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Let thy eyes look straight on, and let thy eyelids go before thy steps.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               Make straight the path for thy feet, and all thy ways shall be established.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=4&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               Decline not to the right hand, nor to the left: turn away thy foot from evil. For the Lord knoweth the ways that are on the right hand: but those are perverse which are on the left hand. But he will make thy courses straight, he will bring forward thy ways in peace.
              </p>
             </td>

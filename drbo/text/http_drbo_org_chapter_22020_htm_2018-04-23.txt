             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               (No prolog)
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Wine is a luxurious thing, and drunkenness riotous: whosoever is delighted therewith shall not be wise.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               As the roaring of a lion, so also is the dread of a king: he that provoketh him, sinneth against his own soul.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               It is an honour for a man to separate himself from quarrels: but all fools are meddling with reproaches.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Because of the cold the sluggard would not plough: he shall beg therefore in the summer, and it shall not be given him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Counsel in the heart of a man is like deep water: but a wise man will draw it out.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Many men are called merciful: but who shall find a faithful man?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               The just that walketh in his simplicity, shall leave behind him blessed children.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               The king, that sitteth on the throne of judgment, scattereth away all evil with his look.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Who can say: My heart is clean, I am pure from sin?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Diverse weights and diverse measures, both are abominable before God.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               By his inclinations a child is known, if his works be clean and right.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               The hearing ear, and the seeing eye, the Lord hath made them both.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Love not sleep, lest poverty oppress thee: open thy eyes, and be filled with bread.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               It is nought, it is nought, saith every buyer: and when he is gone away, then he will boast.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               There is gold, and a multitude of jewels: but the lips of knowledge are a precious vessel.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               Take away the garment of him that is surety for a stranger, and take a pledge from him for strangers.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               The bread of lying is sweet to a man: but afterwards his mouth shall be filled with gravel.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               Designs are strengthened by counsels: and wars are to be managed by governments.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Meddle not with him that revealeth secrets, and walketh deceitfully, and openeth wide his lips.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               He that curseth his father, and mother, his lamp shall be put out in the midst of darkness.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               The inheritance gotten hastily in the beginning, in the end shall be without a blessing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Say not: I will return evil: wait for the Lord and he will deliver thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Diverse weights are an abomination before the Lord: a deceitful balance is not good.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               The steps of man are guided by the Lord: but who is the man that can understand his own way?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               It is ruin to a man to devour holy ones, and after vows to retract.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               A wise king scattereth the wicked, and bringeth over them the wheel.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               The spirit of a man is the lamp of the Lord, which searcheth all the hidden things of the bowels.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               Mercy and truth preserve the king, and his throne is strengthened by clemency.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               The joy of young men is their strength: and the dignity of old men, their grey hairs.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=20&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               The blueness of a wound shall wipe away evils: and stripes in the more inward parts of the belly.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

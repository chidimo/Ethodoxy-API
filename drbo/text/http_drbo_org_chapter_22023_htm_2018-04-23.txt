              <p class="desc">
               (No prolog)
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               When thou shalt sit to eat with a prince, consider diligently what is set before thy face.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               And put a knife to thy throat, if it be so that thou have thy soul in thy own power.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Be not desirous of his meats, in which is the bread of deceit.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Labour not to be rich: but set bounds to thy prudence.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Lift not up thy eyes to riches which thou canst not have: because they shall make themselves wings like those of an eagle, and shall fly towards heaven.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Eat not with an envious man, and desire not his meats:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Because like a soothsayer, and diviner, he thinketh that which he knoweth not. Eat and drink, will he say to thee: and his mind is not with thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               The meats which thou hadst eaten, thou shalt vomit up: and shalt loose thy beautiful words.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Speak not in the ears of fools: because they will despise the instruction of thy speech.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Touch not the bounds of little ones: and enter not into the field of the fatherless:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               For their near kinsman is strong: and he will judge their cause against thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Let thy heart apply itself to instruction: and thy ears to words of knowledge.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Withhold not correction from a child: for if thou strike him with the rod, he shall not die.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Thou shalt beat him with the rod, and deliver his soul from hell.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               My son, if thy mind be wise, my heart shall rejoice with thee:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               And my reins shall rejoice, when thy lips shall speak what is right.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Let not thy heart envy sinners: but be thou in the fear of the Lord all the day long:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               Because thou shalt have hope in the latter end, and thy expectation shall not be taken away.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Hear thou, my son, and be wise: and guide thy mind in the way.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Be not in the feasts of great drinkers, nor in their revellings, who contribute flesh to eat:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Because they that give themselves to drinking, and that club together shall be consumed; and drowsiness shall be clothed with rags.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Hearken to thy father, that beget thee: and despise not thy mother when she is old.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Buy truth, and do not sell wisdom, and instruction, and understanding.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               The father of the just rejoiceth greatly: he that hath begotten a wise son, shall have joy in him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Let thy father, and thy mother be joyful, and let her rejoice that bore thee.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               My son, give me thy heart: and let thy eyes keep my ways.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               For a harlot is a deep ditch: and a strange woman is a narrow pit.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               She lieth in wait in the way as a robber, and him whom she shall see unwary, she will kill.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               Who hath woe? whose father hath woe? who hath contentions? who falls into pits? who hath wounds without cause? who hath redness of eyes?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               Surely they that pass their time in wine, and study to drink of their cups.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               Look not upon the wine when it is yellow, when the colour thereof shineth in the glass: it goeth in pleasantly,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               But in the end, it will bite like a snake, and will spread abroad poison like a basilisk.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               Thy eyes shall behold strange women, and thy heart shall utter perverse things.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               And thou shalt be as one sleeping in the midst of the sea, and as a pilot fast asleep, when the stern is lost.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=23&amp;l=35-#x">
                [35]
               </a>
               And thou shalt say: They have beaten me, but I was not sensible of pain: they drew me, and I felt not: when shall I awake, and find wine again?
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

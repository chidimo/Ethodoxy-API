             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The prayer of Jeremias the prophet.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Remember, O Lord, what is come upon us: consider and behold our reproach.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Our inheritance is turned to aliens: our houses to strangers.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               We are become orphans without a father: our mothers are as widows.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               We have drunk our water for money: we have bought our wood.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               We were dragged by the necks, we were weary and no rest was given us.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               We have given our hand to Egypt, and to the Assyrians, that we might be satisfied with bread.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Our fathers have sinned, and are not: and we have borne their iniquities.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Servants have ruled over us: there was none to redeem us out of their hand.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               We fetched our bread at the peril of our lives, because of the sword in the desert.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Our skin was burnt as an oven, by reason of the violence of the famine.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               They oppressed the women in Sion, and the virgins in the cities of Juda.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               The princes were hanged up by their hand: they did not respect the persons of the ancient.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               They abused the young men indecently: and the children fell under the wood.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               The ancients have ceased from the gates: the young men from the choir of the singers.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               The joy of our heart is ceased, our dancing is turned into mourning.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               The crown is fallen from our head: woe to us, because we have sinned.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Therefore is our heart sorrowful, therefore are our eyes become dim,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               For mount Sion, because it is destroyed, foxes have walked upon it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               But thou, O Lord, shalt remain for ever, thy throne from generation to generation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Why wilt thou forget us for ever? why wilt thou forsake us for a long time?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Convert us, O Lord, to thee, and we shall be converted: renew our days, as from the beginning.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=29&amp;ch=5&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               But thou hast utterly rejected us, thou art exceedingly angry against us.
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The prophet is commanded again to love an adulteress; to signify God's love to the synagogue. The wretched state of the Jews for a long time, till at last they shall be converted.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=33&amp;ch=3&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               And the Lord said to me: Go yet again, and love a woman beloved of her friend, and an adulteress: as the Lord loveth the children of Israel, and they look to strange gods, and love the husks of the grapes.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=33&amp;ch=3&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               And I bought her to me for fifteen pieces of silver, and for a core of barley, and for half a core of barley.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=33&amp;ch=3&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               And I said to her: Thou shalt wait for me many days: thou shalt not play the harlot, and thou shalt be no man's, and I also will wait for thee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=33&amp;ch=3&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               For the children of Israel shall sit many days without king, and without prince, and without sacrifice, and without altar, and without ephod, and without
               <u>
                theraphim
               </u>
               .
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=33&amp;ch=3&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               And after this the children of Israel shall return, and shall seek the Lord their God, and
               <u>
                David their king
               </u>
               : and they shall fear the Lord, and his goodness in the last days.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [4]
               <u>
                "Theraphim"
               </u>
               : Images or representations.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [5]
               <u>
                "David their king"
               </u>
               : That is, Christ, who is of the house of David.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

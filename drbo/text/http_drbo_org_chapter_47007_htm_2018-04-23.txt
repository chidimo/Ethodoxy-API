             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The third part of the sermon on the mount.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Judge not, that you may not be judged,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               For with what judgment you judge, you shall be judged: and with what measure you mete, it shall be measured to you again.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               And why seest thou the mote that is in thy brother's eye; and seest not the beam that is in thy own eye?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Or how sayest thou to thy brother: Let me cast the mote out of thy eye; and behold a beam is in thy own eye?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Thou hypocrite, cast out first the beam in thy own eye, and then shalt thou see to cast out the mote out of thy brother's eye.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Give not that which is holy to dogs; neither cast ye your pearls before swine, lest perhaps they trample them under their feet, and turning upon you, they tear you.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Ask, and it shall be given you: seek, and you shall find: knock, and it shall be opened to you.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               For every one that asketh, receiveth: and he that seeketh, findeth: and to him that knocketh, it shall be opened.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Or what man is there among you, of whom if his son shall ask bread, will he reach him a stone?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Or if he shall ask him a fish, will he reach him a serpent?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               If you then being evil, know how to give good gifts to your children: how much more will your Father who is in heaven, give good things to them that ask him?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               All things therefore whatsoever you would that men should do to you, do you also to them. For this is the law and the prophets.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Enter ye in at the narrow gate: for wide is the gate, and broad is the way that leadeth to destruction, and many there are who go in thereat.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               How narrow is the gate, and strait is the way that leadeth to life: and few there are that find it!
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Beware of false prophets, who come to you in the clothing of sheep, but inwardly they are ravening wolves.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               By their fruits you shall know them. Do men gather grapes of thorns, or figs of thistles?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Even so every good tree bringeth forth good fruit, and the evil tree bringeth forth evil fruit.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               A good tree cannot bring forth evil fruit, neither can an evil tree bring forth good fruit.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Every tree that bringeth not forth good fruit, shall be cut down, and shall be cast into the fire.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Wherefore by their fruits you shall know them.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Not every one that saith to me, Lord, Lord, shall enter into the kingdom of heaven: but he that doth the will of my Father who is in heaven, he shall enter into the kingdom of heaven.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Many will say to me in that day: Lord, Lord, have not we prophesied in thy name, and cast out devils in thy name, and done many miracles in thy name?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               And then will I profess unto them, I never knew you: depart from me, you that work iniquity.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               Every one therefore that heareth these my words, and doth them, shall be likened to a wise man that built his house upon a rock,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               And the rain fell, and the floods came, and the winds blew, and they beat upon that house, and it fell not, for it was founded on a rock.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               And every one that heareth these my words, and doth them not, shall be like a foolish man that built his house upon the sand,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               And the rain fell, and the floods came, and the winds blew, and they beat upon that house, and it fell, and great was the fall thereof.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               And it came to pass when Jesus had fully ended these words, the people were in admiration at his doctrine.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=47&amp;ch=7&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               For he was teaching them as one having power, and not as the scribes and Pharisees.
              </p>

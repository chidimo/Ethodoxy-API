             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               He blames them for going to law before unbelievers. Of sins that exclude from the kingdom of heaven. The evil of fornication.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Dare any of you, having a matter against another, go to be judged before the unjust, and not before the saints?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Know you not that the saints shall judge this world? And if the world shall be judged by you, are you unworthy to judge the smallest matters?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Know you not that we shall judge angels? how much more things of this world?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               If therefore you have judgments of things pertaining to this world, set them to judge, who are the most despised in the church.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               I speak to your shame. Is it so that there is not among you any one wise man, that is able to judge between his brethren?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               But brother goeth to law with brother, and that before unbelievers.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Already indeed there is plainly
               <u>
                a fault
               </u>
               among you, that you have lawsuits one with another. Why do you not rather take wrong? Why do you not rather suffer yourselves to be defrauded?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               But you do wrong and defraud, and that to your brethren.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Know you not that the unjust shall not possess the kingdom of God? Do not err: neither fornicators, nor idolaters, nor adulterers,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Nor the effeminate, nor liers with mankind, nor thieves, nor covetous, nor drunkards, nor railers, nor extortioners, shall possess the kingdom of God.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [7]
               <u>
                "A fault"
               </u>
               : Lawsuits can hardly ever be without a fault, on the one side or the other; and oftentimes on both sides.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               And such some of you were; but you are washed, but you are sanctified, but you are justified in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ, and the Spirit of our God.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               <u>
                All things are lawful
               </u>
               to me, but all things are not expedient. All things are lawful to me, but I will not be brought under the power of any.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Meat for the belly, and the belly for the meats; but God shall destroy both it and them: but the body is not for fornication, but for the Lord, and the Lord for the body.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Now God hath both raised up the Lord, and will raise us up also by his power.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Know you not that your bodies are the members of Christ? Shall I then take the members of Christ, and make them the members of an harlot? God forbid.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [12]
               <u>
                "All things are lawful"
               </u>
               : That is, all indifferent things are indeed lawful, inasmuch as they are not prohibited; but oftentimes they are not expedient; as in the case of lawsuits, etc. And much less would it be expedient to be enslaved by an irregular affection to any thing, how indifferent soever.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               Or know you not, that he who is joined to a harlot, is made one body? For they shall be, saith he, two in one flesh.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               But he who is joined to the Lord, is one spirit.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               Fly fornication. Every sin that a man doth, is without the body; but he that committeth fornication, sinneth against his own body.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Or know you not, that your members are the temple of the Holy Ghost, who is in you, whom you have from God; and you are not your own?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=6&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               For you are bought with a great price. Glorify and bear God in your body.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

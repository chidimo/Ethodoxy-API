           <td class="textarea" valign="top">
            <p class="desc">
             He blames the Galatians for suffering themselves to be imposed upon by new teachers. The apostle's calling.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=1-#x">
              [1]
             </a>
             Paul, an apostle, not of men, neither by man, but by Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead,
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=2-#x">
              [2]
             </a>
             And all the brethren who are with me, to the churches of Galatia.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=3-#x">
              [3]
             </a>
             Grace be to you, and peace from God the Father, and from our Lord Jesus Christ,
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=4-#x">
              [4]
             </a>
             Who gave himself for our sins, that he might deliver us from this present wicked world, according to the will of God and our Father:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=5-#x">
              [5]
             </a>
             To whom is glory for ever and ever. Amen.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=6-#x">
              [6]
             </a>
             I wonder that you are so soon removed from him that called you into the grace of Christ, unto another gospel.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=7-#x">
              [7]
             </a>
             Which is not another, only there are some that trouble you, and would pervert the gospel of Christ.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=8-#x">
              [8]
             </a>
             But though we, or an angel from heaven, preach a gospel to you besides that which we have preached to you, let him be anathema.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=9-#x">
              [9]
             </a>
             As we said before, so now I say again: If any one preach to you a gospel, besides that which you have received, let him be anathema.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=10-#x">
              [10]
             </a>
             For do I now persuade men, or God? Or do I seek to please men? If I yet pleased men, I should not be the servant of Christ.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=11-#x">
              [11]
             </a>
             For I give you to understand, brethren, that the gospel which was preached by me is not according to man.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=12-#x">
              [12]
             </a>
             For neither did I receive it of man, nor did I learn it; but by the revelation of Jesus Christ.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=13-#x">
              [13]
             </a>
             For you have heard of my conversation in time past in the Jews' religion: how that, beyond measure, I persecuted the church of God, and wasted it.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=14-#x">
              [14]
             </a>
             And I made progress in the Jews' religion above many of my equals in my own nation, being more abundantly zealous for the traditions of my fathers.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=15-#x">
              [15]
             </a>
             But when it pleased him, who separated me from my mother's womb, and called me by his grace,
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=16-#x">
              [16]
             </a>
             To reveal his Son in me, that I might preach him among the Gentiles, immediately I condescended not to flesh and blood.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=17-#x">
              [17]
             </a>
             Neither went I to Jerusalem, to the apostles who were before me: but I went into Arabia, and again I returned to Damascus.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=18-#x">
              [18]
             </a>
             Then, after three years, I went to Jerusalem, to see Peter, and I tarried with him fifteen days.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=19-#x">
              [19]
             </a>
             But other of the apostles I saw none, saving James the brother of the Lord.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=20-#x">
              [20]
             </a>
             Now the things which I write to you, behold, before God, I lie not.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=21-#x">
              [21]
             </a>
             Afterwards I came into the regions of Syria and Cilicia.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=22-#x">
              [22]
             </a>
             And I was unknown by face to the churches of Judea, which were in Christ:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=23-#x">
              [23]
             </a>
             But they had heard only: He, who persecuted us in times past, doth now preach the faith which once he impugned:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=1&amp;l=24-#x">
              [24]
             </a>
             And they glorified God in me.
            </p>

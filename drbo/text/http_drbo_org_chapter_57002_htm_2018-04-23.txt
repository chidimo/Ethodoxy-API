             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               He recommends them to unity and humility, and to work out their salvation with fear and trembling.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               If there be therefore any consolation in Christ, if any comfort of charity, if any society of the spirit, if any bowels of commiseration:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Fulfill ye my joy, that you may be of one mind, having the same charity, being of one accord, agreeing in sentiment.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Let nothing be done through contention, neither by vain glory: but in humility, let each esteem others better than themselves:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Each one not considering the things that are his own, but those that are other men's.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               For let this mind be in you, which was also in Christ Jesus:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Who being in the form of God, thought it not robbery to be equal with God:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               But
               <u>
                emptied himself
               </u>
               , taking the form of a servant, being made in the likeness of men, and in habit found as a man.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               He humbled himself, becoming obedient unto death, even to the death of the cross.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               For which cause God also hath exalted him, and hath given him a name which is above all names:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               That in the name of Jesus every knee should bow, of those that are in heaven, on earth, and under the earth:
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [7]
               <u>
                "Emptied himself"
               </u>
               : exinanivit, made himself as of no account.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               And that every tongue should confess that the Lord Jesus Christ is in the glory of God the Father.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Wherefore, my dearly beloved, (as you have always obeyed, not as in my presence only, but much more now in my absence,)
               <u>
                with fear
               </u>
               and trembling work out your salvation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               For it is God who worketh in you, both to will and to accomplish, according to his good will.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               And do ye all things without murmurings and hesitations;
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               That you may be blameless, and sincere children of God, without reproof, in the midst of a crooked and perverse generation; among whom you shine as lights in the world.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [12]
               <u>
                "With fear"
               </u>
               : This is against the false faith, and presumptuous security of modern sectaries.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               Holding forth the word of life to my glory in the day of Christ, because I have not run in vain, nor laboured in vain.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Yea, and if I be made a victim upon the sacrifice and service of your faith, I rejoice, and congratulate with you all.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               And for the selfsame thing do you also rejoice, and congratulate with me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               And I hope in the Lord Jesus to send Timothy unto you shortly, that I also may be of good comfort, when I know the things concerning you.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               For I have no man so of the same mind, who with sincere affection is solicitous for you.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               For all seek the things that are their own; not the things that are Jesus Christ's.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Now know ye the proof of him, that as a son with the father, so hath he served with me in the gospel.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Him therefore I hope to send unto you immediately, so soon as I shall see how it will go with me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               And I trust in the Lord, that I myself also shall come to you shortly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               But I have thought it necessary to send to you Epaphroditus, my brother and fellow labourer, and fellow soldier, but your apostle, and he that hath ministered to my wants.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               For indeed he longed after you all: and was sad, for that you had heard that he was sick.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               For indeed he was sick, nigh unto death; but God had mercy on him; and not only on him, but on me also, lest I should have sorrow upon sorrow.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               Therefore I sent him the more speedily: that seeing him again, you may rejoice, and I may be without sorrow.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               Receive him therefore with all joy in the Lord; and treat with honour such as he is.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=57&amp;ch=2&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               Because for the work of Christ he came to the point of death: delivering his life, that he might fulfill that which on your part was wanting towards my service.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

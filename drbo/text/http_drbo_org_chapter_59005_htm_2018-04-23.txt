             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The day of the Lord shall come when least expected. Exhortations to several duties.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               But of the times and moments, brethren, you need not, that we should write to you;
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               For yourselves know perfectly, that the day of the Lord shall so come, as a thief in the night.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               For when they shall say, peace and security; then shall sudden destruction come upon them, as the pains upon her that is with child, and they shall not escape.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               But you, brethren, are not in darkness, that that day should overtake you as a thief.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               For all you are the children of light, and children of the day: we are not of the night, nor of darkness.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Therefore, let us not sleep, as others do; but let us watch, and be sober.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               For they that sleep, sleep in the night; and they that are drunk, are drunk in the night.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               But let us, who are of the day, be sober, having on the breastplate of faith and charity, and for a helmet the hope of salvation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               For God hath not appointed us unto wrath, but unto the purchasing of salvation by our Lord Jesus Christ,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Who died for us; that, whether we watch or sleep, we may live together with him.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               For which cause comfort one another; and edify one another, as you also do.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               And we beseech you, brethren, to know them who labour among you, and are over you in the Lord, and admonish you:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               That you esteem them more abundantly in charity, for their work's sake. Have peace with them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               And we beseech you, brethren, rebuke
               <u>
                the unquiet
               </u>
               , comfort the feeble minded, support the weak, be patient towards all men.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               See that none render evil for evil to any man; but ever follow that which is good towards each other, and towards all men.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [14]
               <u>
                "The unquiet"
               </u>
               : That is, such as are irregular and disorderly.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               Always rejoice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Pray without ceasing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               In all things give thanks; for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus concerning you all.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Extinguish not the spirit.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Despise not prophecies.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               But prove all things; hold fast that which is good.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               From all appearance of evil refrain yourselves.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               And may the God of peace himself sanctify you in all things; that your whole spirit, and soul, and body, may be preserved blameless in the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               He is faithful who hath called you, who also will do it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Brethren, pray for us.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               Salute all the brethren with a holy kiss.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               I charge you by the Lord, that this epistle be read to all the holy brethren.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=59&amp;ch=5&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you. Amen.
              </p>
             </td>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Isaac is born. Agar and Ismael are cast forth.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               And the Lord visited Sara, as he had promised: and fulfilled what he had spoken.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               And she conceived and bore a son in her old age, at the time that God had foretold her.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               And Abraham called the name of his son, whom Sara bore him,
               <u>
                Isaac
               </u>
               .
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               And he circumcised him the eighth day, as God had commanded him,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               When he was a hundred years old: for at this age of his father was Isaac born.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [3]
               <u>
                "Isaac"
               </u>
               : This word signifies laughter.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               And Sara said: God hath made a laughter for me: whosoever shall hear of it will laugh with me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               And again she said: Who would believe that Abraham should hear that Sara gave suck to a son, whom she bore to him in his old age.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               And the child grew and was weaned: and Abraham made a great feast on the day of his weaning.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               And when Sara had seen the son of Agar the Egyptian playing with Isaac her son, she said to Abraham:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Cast out this bondwoman, and her son: for the son of the bondwoman shall not be heir with my son Isaac.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Abraham took this grievously for his son.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               And God said to him: Let it not seem grievous to thee for the boy, and for thy bondwoman: in all that Sara hath said to thee, hearken to her voice: for in Isaac shall thy seed be called.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               But I will make the son also of the bondwoman a great nation, because he is thy seed.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               So Abraham rose up in the morning, and taking bread and a bottle of water, put it upon her shoulder, and delivered the boy, and sent her away. And she departed, and wandered in the wilderness of Bersabee.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               And when the water in the bottle was spent, she cast the boy under one of the trees that were there.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               And she went her way, and sat over against him a great way off as far as a bow can carry, for she said: I will not see the boy die: and sitting over against, she lifted up her voice and wept.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               And God heard the voice of the boy: and an angel of God called to Agar from heaven, saying: What art thou doing, Agar? fear not: for God hath heard the voice of the boy, from the place wherein he is.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               Arise, take up the boy, and hold him by the hand: for I will make him a great nation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               And God opened her eyes: and she saw a well of water, and went and filled the bottle, and gave the boy to drink.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               And God was with him: and he grew, and dwelt in the wilderness, and became a young man, an archer.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               And he dwelt in the wilderness of Pharan, and his mother took a wife for him out of the land of Egypt.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               At the same time Abimelech, and Phicol the general of his army said to Abraham: God is with thee in all that thou dost.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Swear therefore by God, that thou wilt not hurt me, nor my posterity, nor my stock: but according to the kindness that I have done to thee, thou shalt do to me, and to the land wherein thou hast lived a stranger.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               And Abraham said: I will swear.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               And he reproved Abimelech for a well of water, which his servants had taken away by force.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               And Abimelech answered: I knew not who did this thing: and thou didst not tell me, and I heard not of it till today.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               And Abraham took sheep and oxen and gave them to Abimelech: and both of them made a league.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               And Abraham set apart seven ewe lambs of the flock.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               And Abimelech said to him: What mean these seven ewe lambs which thou hast set apart?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               But he said: Thou shalt take seven ewe lambs at my hand: that they may be a testimony for me, that I dug this well.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               Therefore that place was called
               <u>
                Bersabee
               </u>
               : because there both of them did swear.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               And they made a league for the well of oath.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               And Abimelech, and Phicol the general of his army arose and returned to the land of the Palestines. But Abraham planted a grove in Bersabee, and there called upon the name of the Lord God eternal.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=1&amp;ch=21&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               And he was a sojourner in the land of the Palestines many days.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [31]
               <u>
                "Bersabee"
               </u>
               : That is, the well of oath.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Sophar declares the shortness of the prosperity of the wicked: and their sudden downfall.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Then Sophar the Naamathite answered, and said:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Therefore various thoughts succeed one another in me, and my mind is hurried away to different things.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               The doctrine with which thou reprovest me, I will hear, and the spirit of my understanding shall answer for me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               This I know from the beginning, since man was placed upon the earth,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               That the praise of the wicked is short, and the joy of the hypocrite but for a moment.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               If his pride mount up even to heaven, and his head touch the clouds:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               In the end he shall be destroyed like a dunghill, and they that has seen him shall say: Where is he?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               As a dream that fleeth away he shall not be found, he shall pass as a vision of the night:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               The eyes that had seen him, shall see him no more, neither shall his place any more behold him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               His children shall be oppressed with want, and his hands shall render him his sorrow.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               His bones shall be filled with the vices of his youth, and they shall sleep with him in the dust.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               For when evil shall be sweet in his mouth, he will hide it under his tongue.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               He will spare it, and not leave it, and will hide it in his throat.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               His bread in his belly shall be turned into the gall of asps within him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               The riches which he hath swallowed; he shall vomit up, and God shall draw them out of his belly.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               He shall suck the head of asps, and the viper's tongue shall kill him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               (Let him not see the streams of the river, the brooks of honey and of butter.)
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               He shall be punished for all that he did, and yet shall not be consumed:
               <u>
                according to the multitude of his devices
               </u>
               so also shall he suffer.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Because he broke in and stripped the poor: he hath violently taken away a house which he did not build.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               And yet his belly was not filled: and when he hath the things he coveted, he shall not be able to possess them.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [18]
               <u>
                "According to the multitude of his devices"
               </u>
               : That is, his stratagems to gratify his passions and to oppress and destroy the poor.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               There was nothing left of his meat, and therefore nothing shall continue of his goods:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               When he shall be filled, he shall be straitened, he shall burn, and every sorrow shall fall upon him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               May his belly be filled, that God may send forth the wrath of his indignation upon him, and rain down his war upon him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               He shall flee from weapons of iron, and shall fall upon a bow of brass.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               The sword is drawn out, and cometh forth from its scabbard, and glittereth in his bitterness: the terrible ones shall go and come upon him.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               All darkness is hid in his secret places: a fire that is not kindled shall devour him, he shall be afflicted when left in his tabernacle.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               The heavens shall reveal his iniquity, and the earth shall rise up against him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               The offspring of his house shall be exposed, he shall be pulled down in the day of God's wrath.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=20&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               This is the portion of a wicked man from God, and the inheritance of his doings from the Lord.
              </p>
             </td>

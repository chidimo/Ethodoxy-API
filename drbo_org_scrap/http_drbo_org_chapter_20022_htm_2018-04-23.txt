             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Eliphaz falsely imputes many crimes to Job, but promises him prosperity if he will repent.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Then Eliphaz the Themanite answered, and said:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Can man be compared with God, even though he were of perfect knowledge?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               What doth it profit God if thou be just? or what dost thou give him if thy way be unspotted?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Shall he reprove thee for fear, and come with thee into judgment:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               And not for thy manifold wickedness, and thy infinite iniquities?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               For thou hast taken away the pledge of thy brethren without cause, and stripped the naked of their clothing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Thou hast not given water to the weary, thou hast withdrawn bread from the hungry.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               In the strength of thy arm thou didst possess the land, and being the most mighty thou holdest it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Thou hast sent widows away empty, and the arms of the fatherless thou hast broken in pieces.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Therefore art thou surrounded with snares, and sudden fear troubleth thee.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               And didst thou think that thou shouldst not see darkness, and that thou shouldst not be covered with the violence of overflowing waters?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Dost not thou think that God is higher than heaven, and is elevated above the height of the stars?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               And thou sayst: What doth God know? and he judgeth as it were through a mist.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               The clouds are his covert, and he doth not consider our things, and he walketh about the poles of heaven.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Dost thou desire to keep the path of ages, which wicked men have trodden?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               Who were taken away before their time, and a flood hath overthrown their foundation.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Who said to God: Depart from us: and looked upon the Almighty as if he could do nothing:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               Whereas he had filled their houses with good things: whose way of thinking be far from me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               The just shall see, and shall rejoice, and the innocent shall laugh them to scorn.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Is not their exaltation cut down, and hath not fire devoured the remnants of them?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Submit thyself then to him, and be at peace: and thereby thou shalt have the best fruits.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Receive the law of his mouth, and lay up his words in thy heart.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               If thou wilt return to the Almighty, thou shalt be built up, and shalt put away iniquity far from thy tabernacle.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               He shall give for earth flint, and for flint torrents of gold.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               And the Almighty shall be against thy enemies, and silver shall be heaped together for thee.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               Then shalt thou abound in delights in the Almighty, and shalt lift up thy face to God.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               Thou shalt pray to him, and he will hear thee, and thou shalt pay vows.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               Thou shalt decree a thing, and it I shall come to thee, and light shall shine in thy ways.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               For he that hath been humbled, shall be in glory: and he that shall bow down his eyes, he shall be saved.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=22&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               The innocent shall be saved, and he shall be saved by the cleanness of his hands.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

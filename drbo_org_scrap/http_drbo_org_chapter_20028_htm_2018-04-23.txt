             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Man's industry searcheth out many things: true wisdom is taught by God alone.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Silver hath beginnings of its veins, and gold hath a place wherein it is melted.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Iron is taken out of the earth, and stone melted with heat is turned into brass.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               He hath set a time for darkness, and the end of all things he considereth, the stone also that is in the dark and the shadow of death.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               The flood divideth from the people that are on their journey, those whom the food of the needy man hath forgotten, and who cannot be come at.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               The land, out of which bread grew in its place, hath been overturned with fire.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               The stones of it are the place of sapphires, and the clods of it are gold.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               The bird hath not known the path, neither hath the eye of the vulture beheld it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               The children of the merchants have not trodden it, neither hath the lioness passed by it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               He hath stretched forth his hand to the flint, he hath overturned mountains from the roots.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               In the rocks he hath cut out rivers, and his eye hath seen every precious thing.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               The depths also of rivers he hath searched, and hidden things he hath brought forth to light.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               But where is wisdom to be found, and where is the place of understanding?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Man knoweth not the price thereof, neither is it found in the land of them that live in delights.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               The depth saith: It is not in me: and the sea saith: It is not with me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               The finest gold shall not purchase it, neither shall silver be weighed in exchange for it.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               It shall not be compared with the dyed colours of India, or with the most precious stone sardonyx, or the sapphire.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Gold or crystal cannot equal it, neither shall any vessels of gold be changed for it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               High and eminent things shall not be mentioned in comparison of it: but wisdom is drawn out of secret places.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               The topaz of Ethiopia shall not be equal to it, neither shall it be compared to the cleanest dyeing.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Whence then cometh wisdom? and where is the place of understanding?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               It is hid from the eyes of all living. and the fowls of the air know it not.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Destruction and death have said: With our ears we have heard the fame thereof.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               God understandeth the way of it, and he knoweth the place thereof.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               For he beholdeth the ends of the world: and looketh on all things that are under heaven.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Who made a weight for the winds and weighed the waters by measure.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               When he gave a law for the rain, and a way for the sounding storms.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               Then he saw it, and declared, and prepared, and searched it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=20&amp;ch=28&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               And he said to man: Behold the fear of the Lord, that is wisdom: and to depart from evil, is understanding.
              </p>

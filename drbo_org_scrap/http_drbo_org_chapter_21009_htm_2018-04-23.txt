             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Confitebor tibi, Domine. The church praiseth God for his protection against her enemies.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Unto the end, for
               <u>
                the hidden things of the Son
               </u>
               . A psalm for David.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               I will give praise to thee, O Lord, with my whole heart: I will relate all thy wonders.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               I will be glad and rejoice in thee: I will sing to thy name, O thou most high.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               When my enemy shall be turned back: they shall be weakened and perish before thy face.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               For thou hast maintained my judgment and my cause: thou hast sat on the throne, who judgest justice.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [1]
               <u>
                "The hidden things of the Son"
               </u>
               : The humility and sufferings of Christ, the Son of God; and of good Christians, who are his sons by adoption; are called hidden things, with regard to the children of this world, who know not the value and merit of them.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Thou hast rebuked the Gentiles, and the wicked one hath perished: thou hast blotted out their name for ever and ever.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               The swords of the enemy have failed unto the end: and their cities thou hast destroyed. Their memory hath perished with a noise.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               But the Lord remaineth for ever. He hath prepared his throne in judgment:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               And he shall judge the world in equity, he shall judge the people in justice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               And the Lord is become a refuge for the poor: a helper in due time in tribulation.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               And let them trust in thee who know thy name: for thou hast not forsaken them that seek thee, O Lord.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Sing ye to the Lord, who dwelleth in Sion: declare his ways among the Gentiles:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               For requiring their blood he hath remembered them: he hath not forgotten the cry of the poor.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Have mercy on me, O Lord: see my humiliation which I suffer from my enemies.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Thou that liftest me up from the gates of death, that I may declare all thy praises in the gates of the daughter of Sion.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               I will rejoice in thy salvation: the Gentiles have stuck fast in the destruction which they have prepared. Their foot hath been taken in the very snare which they hid.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               The Lord shall be known when he executeth judgments: the sinner hath been caught in the works of his own hands.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               The wicked shall be turned into hell, all the nations that forget God.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               For the poor man shall not be forgotten to the end: the patience of the poor shall not perish for ever.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Arise, O Lord, let not man be strengthened: let the Gentiles be judged in thy sight.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Appoint, O Lord, a lawgiver over them: that the Gentiles may know themselves to be but men.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Why, O Lord, hast thou retired afar off? why dost thou slight us in our wants, in the time of trouble?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Whilst the wicked man is proud, the poor is set on fire: they are caught in the counsels which they devise.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               For the sinner is praised in the desires of his soul: and the unjust man is blessed.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               The sinner hath provoked the Lord according to the multitude of his wrath he will not seek him:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               God is not before his eyes: his ways are filthy at all times. Thy judgments are removed from his sight: he shall rule over all his enemies.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               For he hath said in his heart: I shall not be moved from generation to generation, and shall be without evil.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               His mouth is full of cursing, and of bitterness, and of deceit: under his tongue are labour and sorrow.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               He sitteth in ambush with the rich in private places, that he may kill the innocent.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               His eyes are upon the poor man: He lieth in wait in secret like a lion in his den. He lieth in ambush that he may catch the poor man: to catch the poor, whilst he draweth him to him.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               In his net he will bring him down, he will crouch and fall, when he shall have power over the poor.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               For he hath said in his heart: God hath forgotten, he hath turned away his face not to see to the end.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               Arise, O Lord God, let thy hand be exalted: forget not the poor.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               Wherefore hath the wicked provoked God? for he hath said in his heart: He will not require it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=35-#x">
                [35]
               </a>
               Thou seest it, for thou considerest labour and sorrow: that thou mayst deliver them into thy hands. To thee is the poor man left: thou wilt be a helper to the orphan.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=36-#x">
                [36]
               </a>
               Break thou the arm of the sinner and of the malignant: his sin shall be sought, and shall not be found.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=37-#x">
                [37]
               </a>
               The Lord shall reign to eternity, yea, for ever and ever: ye Gentiles shall perish from his land.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=38-#x">
                [38]
               </a>
               The Lord hath heard the desire of the poor: thy ear hath heard the preparation of their heart.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=21&amp;ch=9&amp;l=39-#x">
                [39]
               </a>
               To judge for the fatherless and for the humble, that man may no more presume to magnify himself upon earth.
              </p>

           <td class="textarea" valign="top">
            <p class="desc">
             The use and end of the proverbs. An exhortation to flee the company of the wicked: and to hearken to the voice of wisdom.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=1-#x">
              [1]
             </a>
             The parables of Solomon, the son of David, king of Israel.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=2-#x">
              [2]
             </a>
             To know wisdom, and instruction:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=3-#x">
              [3]
             </a>
             To understand the words of prudence: and to receive the instruction of doctrine, justice, and judgment, and equity:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=4-#x">
              [4]
             </a>
             To give subtilty to little ones, to the young man knowledge and understanding.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=5-#x">
              [5]
             </a>
             A wise man shall hear and shall be wiser: and he that understandeth, shall possess governments.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=6-#x">
              [6]
             </a>
             He shall understand a parable, and the interpretation, the words of the wise, and their mysterious sayings.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=7-#x">
              [7]
             </a>
             The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom. Fools despise wisdom and instruction.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=8-#x">
              [8]
             </a>
             My son, hear the instruction of thy father, and forsake not the law of thy mother:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=9-#x">
              [9]
             </a>
             That grace may be added to thy head, and a chain of gold to thy neck.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=10-#x">
              [10]
             </a>
             My son, if sinners shall entice thee, consent not to them.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=11-#x">
              [11]
             </a>
             If they shall say: Come with us, let us lie in wait for blood, let us hide snares for the innocent without cause:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=12-#x">
              [12]
             </a>
             Let us swallow him up alive like hell, and whole as one that goeth down into the pit.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=13-#x">
              [13]
             </a>
             We shall find all precious substance, we shall fill our houses with spoils.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=14-#x">
              [14]
             </a>
             Cast in thy lot with us, let us all have one purse.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=15-#x">
              [15]
             </a>
             My son, walk not thou with them, restrain thy foot from their paths.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=16-#x">
              [16]
             </a>
             For their feet run to evil, and make haste to shed blood.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=17-#x">
              [17]
             </a>
             But a net is spread in vain before the eyes of them that have wings.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=18-#x">
              [18]
             </a>
             And they themselves lie in wait for their own blood, and practise deceits against their own souls.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=19-#x">
              [19]
             </a>
             So the wage of every covetous man destroy the souls of the possessors.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=20-#x">
              [20]
             </a>
             Wisdom preacheth abroad, she uttereth her voice in the streets:
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=21-#x">
              [21]
             </a>
             At the head of multitudes she crieth out, in the entrance of the gates of the city she uttereth her words, saying:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=22-#x">
              [22]
             </a>
             O children, how long will you love childishness, and fools covet those things which are hurtful to themselves, and the unwise hate knowledge?
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=23-#x">
              [23]
             </a>
             Turn ye at my reproof: behold I will utter my spirit to you, and will shew you my words.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=24-#x">
              [24]
             </a>
             Because I called, and you refused: I stretched out my hand, and there was none that regarded.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=25-#x">
              [25]
             </a>
             You have despised all my counsel, and have neglected my reprehensions.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=26-#x">
              [26]
             </a>
             I also will laugh in your destruction, and will mock when that shall come to you which you feared.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=27-#x">
              [27]
             </a>
             When sudden calamity shall fall on you, and destruction, as a tempest, shall be at hand: when tribulation and distress shall come upon you:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=28-#x">
              [28]
             </a>
             Then shall they call upon me, and I will not hear: they shall rise in the morning and shall not find me:
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=29-#x">
              [29]
             </a>
             Because they have hated instruction and received not the fear of the Lord,
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=30-#x">
              [30]
             </a>
             Nor consented to my counsel, but despised all my reproof.
            </p>
            <p>
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=31-#x">
              [31]
             </a>
             Therefore they shall eat the fruit of their own way, and shall be filled with their own devices.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=32-#x">
              [32]
             </a>
             The turning away of little ones shall kill them, and the prosperity of fools shall destroy them.
             <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=1&amp;l=33-#x">
              [33]
             </a>
             But he that shall hear me, shall rest without terror, and shall enjoy abundance, without fear of evils.
            </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The preaching of wisdom. Her excellence.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Doth not wisdom cry aloud, and prudence put forth her voice?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Standing in the top of the highest places by the way, in the midst of the paths.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Beside the gates of the city, in the very doors she speaketh, saying:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               O ye men, to you I call, and my voice is to the sons of men.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               O little ones, understand subtilty, and ye unwise, take notice.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Hear, for I will speak of great things: and my lips shall be opened to preach right things.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               My mouth shall meditate truth, and my lips shall hate wickedness.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               All my words are just, there is nothing wicked nor perverse in them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               They are right to them that understand, and just to them that find knowledge.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Receive my instruction, and not money: choose knowledge rather than gold.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               For wisdom is better than all the most precious things: and whatsoever may be desired cannot be compared to it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               I wisdom dwell in counsel, and am present in learned thoughts.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               The fear of the Lord hateth evil: I hate arrogance, and pride, and every wicked way, and a mouth with a double tongue.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Counsel and equity is mine, prudence is mine, strength is mine.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               By me kings reign, and lawgivers decree just things,
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               By me princes rule, and the mighty decree justice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               I love them that love me: and they that in the morning early watch for me, shall find me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               With me are riches and glory, glorious riches and justice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               For my fruit is better than gold and the precious stone, and my blossoms than choice silver.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               I walk in the way of justice, in the midst of the paths of judgment,
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               That I may enrich them that love me, and may fill their treasures.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               The Lord possessed me in the beginning of his ways, before he made any thing from the beginning.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               I was set up from eternity, and of old before the earth was made.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               The depths were not as yet, and I was already conceived. neither had the fountains of waters as yet sprung out:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               The mountains with their huge bulk had not as yet been established: before the hills I was brought forth:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               He had not yet made the earth, nor the rivers, nor the poles of the world.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               When he prepared the heavens, I was present: when with a certain law and compass he enclosed the depths:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               When he established the sky above, and poised the fountains of waters:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               When he compassed the sea with its bounds, and set a law to the waters that they should not pass their limits: when be balanced the foundations of the earth;
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               I was with him forming all things: and was delighted every day, playing before him at all times;
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               Playing in the world: and my delights were to be with the children of men.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               Now therefore, ye children, hear me: Blessed are they that keep my ways.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               Hear instruction and be wise, and refuse it not.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               Blessed is the man that heareth me, and that watcheth daily at my gates, and waiteth at the posts of my doors.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=35-#x">
                [35]
               </a>
               He that shall find me, shall find life, and shall have salvation from the Lord:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=8&amp;l=36-#x">
                [36]
               </a>
               But he that shall sin against me, shall hurt his own soul. All that hate me love death.
              </p>

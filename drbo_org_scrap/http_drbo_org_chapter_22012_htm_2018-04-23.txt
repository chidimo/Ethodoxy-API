             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               (No prolog)
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               He that loveth correction, loveth knowledge: but he that hateth reproof is foolish.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               He that is good, shall draw grace from the Lord: but he that trusteth in his own devices doth wickedly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Men shall not be strengthened by wickedness: and the root of the just shall not be moved.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               A diligent woman is a crown to her husband: and she that doth things worthy of confusion, is a rottenness in his bones.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               The thoughts of the just are judgments: and the counsels of the wicked are deceitful.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               The words of the wicked lie in wait for blood: the mouth of the just shall deliver them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Turn the wicked, and they shall not be: but the house of the just shall stand firm.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               A man shall be known by his learning: but he that is vain and foolish, shall be exposed to contempt.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Better is the poor man that provideth for himself, than he that is glorious and wanteth bread.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               The just regardeth the lives of his beasts: but the bowels of the wicked are cruel.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               He that tilleth his land shall be satisfied with bread: but he that pursueth idleness is very foolish. He that is delighted in passing his time over wine, leaveth a reproach in his strong holds.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               The desire of the wicked is the fortification of evil men: but the root of the just shall prosper.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               For the sins of the lips ruin draweth nigh to the evil man: but the just shall escape out of distress.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               By the fruit of his own mouth shall a man be filled with good things, and according to the works of his hands it shall be repaid him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               The way of a fool is right in his own eyes: but he that is wise hearkeneth unto counsels.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               A fool immediately sheweth his anger: but he that dissembleth injuries is wise.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               He that speaketh that which he knoweth, sheweth forth justice: but he that lieth, is a deceitful witness.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               There is that promiseth, and is pricked as it were with a sword of conscience: but the tongue of the wise is health.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               The lip of truth shall be steadfast for ever: but he that is a hasty witness, frameth a lying tongue.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Deceit is in the heart of them that think evil things: but joy followeth them that take counsels of peace.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Whatsoever shall befall the just man. it shall not make him sad: but the wicked shall be filled with mischief.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Lying lips are an abomination to the Lord: but they that deal faithfully please him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               A cautious man concealeth knowledge: and the heart of fools publisheth folly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               The hand of the valiant shall bear rule: but that which is slothful, shall be under tribute.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Grief in the heart of a man shall bring him low, but with a good word he shall be made glad.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               He that neglecteth a loss for the sake of a friend, is just: but the way of the wicked shall deceive them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               The deceitful man shall not find gain: but the substance of a just man shall be precious gold.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=12&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               In the path of justice is life: but the by-way leadeth to death.
              </p>
             </td>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               (No prolog)
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               A wise son heareth the doctrine of his father: but he that is a scorner, beareth not when he is reproved.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Of the fruit of his own mouth shall a man be filled with good things: but the soul of transgressors is wicked.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               He that keepeth his mouth, keepeth his soul: but he that hath no guard on his speech shall meet with evils.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               The sluggard willeth and willeth not: but the soul of them that work, shall be made fat.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               The just shall hate a lying word: but the wicked confoundeth, and shall be confounded.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Justice keepeth the way of the innocent: but wickedness overthroweth the sinner.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               One is as it were rich, when he hath nothing: and another is as it were poor, when he hath great riches.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               The ransom of a man's life are his riches: but he that is poor beareth not reprehension.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               The light of the just giveth joy: but the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Among the proud there are always contentions: but they that do all things with counsel, are ruled by wisdom.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Substance got in haste shall be diminished: but that which by little and little is gathered with the hand shall increase.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Hope that is deferred afflicteth the soul: desire when it cometh is a tree of life.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Whosoever speaketh ill of any thing, bindeth himself for the time to come: but he that feareth the commandment, shall dwell in peace. Deceitful souls go astray in sins: the just are merciful, and shew mercy.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               The law of the wise is a fountain of life, that he may decline from the ruin of death.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Good instruction shall give grace: in the way of scorners is a deep pit.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               The prudent man doth all things with counsel: but he that is a fool, layeth open his folly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               The messenger of the wicked shall fall into mischief: but a faithful ambassador is health.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               Poverty and shame to him that refuseth instruction: but he that yieldeth to reproof, shall be glorified.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               The desire that is accomplished, delighteth the soul: fools hate them that flee from evil things.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               He that walketh with the wise, shall be wise: a friend of fools shall become like to them.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Evil pursueth sinners: and to the just good shall be repaid.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               The good man leaveth heirs, sons, and grandsons: and the substance of the sinner is kept for the just.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Much food is in the tillage of fathers: but for others it is gathered with out judgment.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               He that spareth the rod hateth his son: but he that loveth him correcteth him betimes.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=13&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               The just eateth and filleth his soul: but the belly of the wicked is never to be filled.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               (No prolog)
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Seek not to be like evil men, neither desire to be with them:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Because their mind studieth robberies, and their lips speak deceits.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               By wisdom the house shall be built, and by prudence it shall be strengthened.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               By instruction the storerooms shall be filled with all precious and most beautiful wealth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               A wise man is strong: and a knowing man, stout and valiant.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Because war is managed by due ordering: and there shall be safety where there are many counsels.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Wisdom is too high for a fool, in the gate he shall not open his mouth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               He that deviseth to do evils, shall be called a fool.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               The thought of a fool is sin: and the detracter is the abomination of men.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               If thou lose hope being weary in the day of distress, thy strength shall be diminished.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Deliver them that are led to death: and those that are drawn to death forbear not to deliver.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               If thou say: I have not strength enough: he that seeth into the heart, he understandeth, and nothing deceiveth the keeper of thy soul, and he shall render to a man according to his works.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Eat honey, my son, because it is good, and the honeycomb most sweet to thy throat:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               So also is the doctrine of wisdom to thy soul: which when thou hast found, thou shalt have hope in the end, and thy hope shall not perish.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Lie not in wait, nor seek after wickedness in the house of the just, nor spoil his rest.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               For a just man shall fall seven times and shall rise again: but the wicked shall fall down into evil.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               When thy enemy shall fall, be not glad, and in his ruin let not thy heart rejoice:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               Lest the Lord see, and it displease him, and he turn away his wrath from him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Contend not with the wicked, nor seek to be like the ungodly:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               For evil men have no hope of things to come, and the lamp of the wicked shall be put out.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               My son, fear the Lord and the king: and have nothing to do with detracters.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               For their destruction shall rise suddenly: and who knoweth the ruin of both?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               These things also to the wise: It is not good to have respect to persons in judgment.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               They that say to the wicked man: Thou art just: shall be cursed by the people, and the tribes shall abhor them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               They that rebuke him, shall be praised: and a blessing shall come upon them.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               He shall kiss the lips, who answereth right words.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               Prepare thy work without, and diligently till thy ground: that afterward thou mayst build thy house.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               Be not witness without cause against thy neighbour: and deceive not any man with thy lips.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               Say not: I will do to him as he hath done to me: I will render to every one according to his work.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               I passed by the field of the slothful man, and by the vineyard of the foolish man:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               And behold it was all filled with nettles, and thorns had covered the face thereof, and the stone wall was broken down.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               Which when I had seen, I laid it up in my heart, and by the example I received instruction.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               Thou wilt sleep a little, said I, thou wilt slumber a little, thou wilt fold thy hands a little to rest:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=24&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               And poverty shall come to thee as a runner, and beggary as an armed man.
              </p>

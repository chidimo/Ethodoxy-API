             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               (No prolog)
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               The man that with a stiff neck despiseth him that reproveth him, shall suddenly be destroyed: and health shall not follow him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               When just men increase, the people shall rejoice: when the wicked shall bear rule, the people shall mourn.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               A man that loveth wisdom, rejoiceth his father: but he that maintaineth harlots, shall squander away his substance.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               A just king setteth up the land: a covetous man shall destroy it.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               A man that speaketh to his friend with flattering and dissembling words, spreadeth a net for his feet.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               A snare shall entangle the wicked man when he sinneth: and the just shall praise and rejoice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               The just taketh notice of the cause of the poor: the wicked is void of knowledge.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Corrupt men bring a city to ruin: but wise men turn away wrath.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               If a wise man contend with a fool, whether he be angry or laugh, he shall find no rest.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Bloodthirsty men hate the upright: but just men seek his soul.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               A fool uttereth all his mind: a wise man deferreth, and keepeth it till afterwards.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               A prince that gladly heareth lying words, hath all his servants wicked.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               The poor man and the creditor have met one another: the Lord is the enlightener of them both.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               The king that judgeth the poor in truth, his throne shall be established for ever.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               The rod and reproof give wisdom: but the child that is left to his own will bringeth his mother to shame.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               When the wicked are multiplied, crimes shall be multiplied: but the just shall see their downfall.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Instruct thy son, and he shall refresh thee, and shall give delight to thy soul.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               When prophecy shall fail, the people shall be scattered abroad: but he that keepeth the law is blessed.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               A slave will not be corrected by words: because he understandeth what thou sayest, and will not answer.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Hast thou seen a man hasty to speak? folly is rather to be looked for, than his amendment.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               He that nourisheth his servant delicately from his childhood, afterwards shall find him stubborn.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               A passionate man provoketh quarrels: and he that is easily stirred up to wrath, shall be more prone to sin.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Humiliation followeth the proud: and glory shall uphold the humble of spirit.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               He that is partaker with a thief, hateth his own soul: he heareth one putting him to his oath, and discovereth not.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               He that feareth man, shall quickly fall: he that trusteth in the Lord, shall be set on high.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               Many seek the face of the prince: but the judgment of every one cometh forth from the Lord.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=22&amp;ch=29&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               The just abhor the wicked man: and the wicked loathe them that are in the right way. The son that keepeth the word, shall be free from destruction.
              </p>
             </td>

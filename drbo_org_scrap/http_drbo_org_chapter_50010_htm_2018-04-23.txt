             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Christ is the door and the good shepherd. He and his Father are one.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Amen, amen I say to you: He that entereth not by the door into the sheepfold, but climbeth up another way, the same is a thief and a robber.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               But he that entereth in by the door is the shepherd of the sheep.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               To him the porter openeth; and the sheep hear his voice: and he calleth his own sheep by name, and leadeth them out.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               And when he hath let out his own sheep, he goeth before them: and the sheep follow him, because they know his voice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               But a stranger they follow not, but fly from him, because they know not the voice of strangers.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               This proverb Jesus spoke to them. But they understood not what he spoke to them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Jesus therefore said to them again: Amen, amen I say to you, I am the door of the sheep.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               All others, as many as have come, are thieves and robbers: and the sheep heard them not.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               I am the door. By me, if any man enter in, he shall be saved: and he shall go in, and go out, and shall find pastures.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               The thief cometh not, but for to steal, and to kill, and to destroy. I am come that they may have life, and may have it more abundantly.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               I am the good shepherd. The good shepherd giveth his life for his sheep.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               But the hireling, and he that is not the shepherd, whose own the sheep are not, seeth the wolf coming, and leaveth the sheep, and flieth: and the wolf catcheth, and scattereth the sheep:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               And the hireling flieth, because he is a hireling: and he hath no care for the sheep.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               I am the good shepherd; and I know mine, and mine know me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               As the Father knoweth me, and I know the Father: and I lay down my life for my sheep.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               And other sheep I have, that are not of this fold: them also I must bring, and they shall hear my voice, and there shall be one fold and one shepherd.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Therefore doth the Father love me: because I lay down my life, that I may take it again.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               No man taketh it away from me: but I lay it down of myself, and I have power to lay it down: and I have power to take it up again. This commandment have I received of my Father.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               A dissension rose again among the Jews for these words.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               And many of them said: He hath a devil, and is mad: why hear you him?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Others said: These are not the words of one that hath a devil: Can a devil open the eyes of the blind?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               And it was the feast of the dedication at Jerusalem: and it was winter.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               And Jesus walked in the temple, in Solomon's porch.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               The Jews therefore came round about him, and said to him: How long dost thou hold our souls in suspense? If thou be the Christ, tell us plainly.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               Jesus answered them: I speak to you, and you believe not: the works that I do in the name of my Father, they give testimony of me.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               But you do not believe, because you are not of my sheep.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               My sheep hear my voice: and I know them, and they follow me.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               And I give them life everlasting; and they shall not perish for ever, and no man shall pluck them out of my hand.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               That which my Father hath given me, is greater than all: and no one can snatch them out of the hand of my Father.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=30-#x">
                [30]
               </a>
               <u>
                I and the Father are one
               </u>
               .
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [30]
               <u>
                "I and the Father are one"
               </u>
               : That is, one divine nature, but two distinct persons.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=31-#x">
                [31]
               </a>
               The Jews then took up stones to stone him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=32-#x">
                [32]
               </a>
               Jesus answered them: Many good works I have shewed you from my Father; for which of these works do you stone me?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=33-#x">
                [33]
               </a>
               The Jews answered him: For a good work we stone thee not, but for blasphemy; and because that thou, being a man, maketh thyself God.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=34-#x">
                [34]
               </a>
               Jesus answered them: Is it not written in your law: I said you are gods?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=35-#x">
                [35]
               </a>
               If he called them gods, to whom the word of God was spoken, and the scripture cannot be broken;
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=36-#x">
                [36]
               </a>
               Do you say of him whom the Father hath sanctified and sent into the world: Thou blasphemest, because I said, I am the Son of God?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=37-#x">
                [37]
               </a>
               If I do not the works of my Father, believe me not.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=38-#x">
                [38]
               </a>
               But if I do, though you will not believe me, believe the works: that you may know and believe that the Father is in me, and I in the Father.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=39-#x">
                [39]
               </a>
               They sought therefore to take him; and he escaped out of their hands.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=40-#x">
                [40]
               </a>
               And he went again beyond the Jordan, into that place where John was baptizing first; and there he abode.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=41-#x">
                [41]
               </a>
               And many resorted to him, and they said: John indeed did no sign.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=50&amp;ch=10&amp;l=42-#x">
                [42]
               </a>
               But all things whatsoever John said of this man, were true. And many believed in him.
              </p>

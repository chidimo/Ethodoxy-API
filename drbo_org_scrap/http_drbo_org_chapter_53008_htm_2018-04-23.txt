             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Though an idol be nothing, yet things offered up to idols are not to be eaten, for fear of scandal.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Now concerning those things that are sacrificed to idols, we know that we all have knowledge.
               <u>
                Knowledge puffeth up
               </u>
               ; but charity edifieth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               And if any man think that he knoweth any thing, he hath not yet known as he ought to know.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               But if any man love God, the same is known by him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               But as for the meats that are sacrificed to idols, we know that an idol is nothing in the world, and that there is no God but one.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               For although there be that are called gods, either in heaven or on earth (for there be
               <u>
                gods many
               </u>
               , and lords many).
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [1]
               <u>
                "Knowledge puffeth up"
               </u>
               : Knowledge, without charity and humility, serveth only to puff persons up.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [5]
               <u>
                "Gods many"
               </u>
               : Reputed for such among the heathens.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Yet to us there is but one God, the Father, of whom are all things, and we unto him; and one Lord Jesus Christ, by whom are all things, and we by him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               But there is not knowledge in every one. For some until this present, with conscience of the idol: eat as a thing sacrificed to an idol, and their conscience, being weak, is defiled.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               But meat doth not commend us to God. For neither, if we eat, shall we have the more; nor, if we eat not, shall we have the less.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               But take heed lest perhaps this your liberty become a stumblingblock to the weak.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               For if a man see him that hath knowledge sit at meat in the idol's temple, shall not his conscience, being weak, be emboldened to eat those things which are sacrificed to idols?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               And through thy knowledge shall the weak brother perish, for whom Christ hath died?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Now when you sin thus against the brethren, and wound their weak conscience, you sin against Christ.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=53&amp;ch=8&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Wherefore,
               <u>
                if meat scandalize
               </u>
               my brother, I will never eat flesh, lest I should scandalize my brother.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [13]
               <u>
                "If meat scandalize"
               </u>
               : That is, if my eating cause my brother to sin.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The Spirit, and the blessing promised to Abraham cometh not by the law, but by faith.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               O senseless Galatians, who hath bewitched you that you should not obey the truth, before whose eyes Jesus Christ hath been set forth, crucified among you?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               This only would I learn of you: Did you receive the Spirit by the works of the law, or by the hearing of faith?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               Are you so foolish, that, whereas you began in the Spirit, you would now be made perfect by the flesh?
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               Have you suffered so great things in vain? If it be yet in vain.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               He therefore who giveth to you the Spirit, and worketh miracles among you; doth he do it by the works of the law, or by the hearing of the faith?
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               As it is written: Abraham believed God, and it was reputed to him unto justice.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               Know ye therefore, that they who are of faith, the same are the children of Abraham.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               And the scripture, foreseeing, that God justifieth the Gentiles by faith, told unto Abraham before: In thee shall all nations be blessed.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               Therefore they that are of faith, shall be blessed with faithful Abraham.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               For as many as are of the works of the law, are under a curse. For it is written: Cursed is every one, that abideth not in all things, which are written in the book of the law to do them.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               But that in the law no man is justified with God, it is manifest: because the just man liveth by faith.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               But the law is not of faith: but, He that doth those things, shall live in them.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Christ hath redeemed us from the curse of the law, being made a curse for us: for it is written: Cursed is every one that hangeth on a tree:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               That the blessing of Abraham might come on the Gentiles through Christ Jesus: that we may receive the promise of the Spirit by faith.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               Brethren (I speak after the manner of man,) yet a man's testament, if it be confirmed, no man despiseth, nor addeth to it.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               To Abraham were the promises made and to his seed. He saith not, And to his seeds, as of many: but as of one, And to thy seed, which is Christ.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               Now this I say, that the testament which was confirmed by God, the law which was made after four hundred and thirty years, doth not disannul, to make the promise of no effect.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               For if the inheritance be of the law, it is no more of promise. But God gave it to Abraham by promise.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               Why then was the law? It was set
               <u>
                because of transgressions
               </u>
               , until the seed should come, to whom he made the promise, being
               <u>
                ordained by angels
               </u>
               in the hand of a mediator.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               Now a mediator is not of one: but God is one.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [19]
               <u>
                "Because of transgressions"
               </u>
               : To restrain them from sin, by fear and threats.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [19]
               <u>
                "Ordained by angels"
               </u>
               : The law was delivered by angels, speaking in the name and person of God to Moses, who was the mediator, on this occasion, between God and the people.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               Was the law then against the promises of God? God forbid. For if there had been a law given which could give life, verily justice should have been by the law.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               But the scripture
               <u>
                hath concluded all under sin
               </u>
               , that the promise, by the faith of Jesus Christ, might be given to them that believe.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               But before the faith came, we were kept under the law shut up, unto that faith which was to be revealed.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               Wherefore the law was our
               <u>
                pedagogue
               </u>
               in Christ, that we might be justified by faith.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=25-#x">
                [25]
               </a>
               But after the faith is come, we are no longer under a pedagogue.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [22]
               <u>
                "Hath concluded all under sin"
               </u>
               : that is, hath declared all to be under sin, from which they could not be delivered but by faith in Jesus Christ, the promised seed.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [24]
               <u>
                "Pedagogue"
               </u>
               : That is, schoolmaster, conductor, or instructor.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=26-#x">
                [26]
               </a>
               For you are all the children of God by faith, in Christ Jesus.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=27-#x">
                [27]
               </a>
               For as many of you as have been baptized in Christ, have put on Christ.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=28-#x">
                [28]
               </a>
               There is
               <u>
                neither Jew
               </u>
               nor Greek: there is neither bond nor free: there is neither male nor female. For you are all one in Christ Jesus.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=55&amp;ch=3&amp;l=29-#x">
                [29]
               </a>
               And if you be Christ's, then are you the seed of Abraham, heirs according to the promise.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [28]
               <u>
                "Neither Jew"
               </u>
               : That is, no distinction of Jew, etc.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               Duties of children and servants. The Christian's armour.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               Children, obey your parents in the Lord, for this is just.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               Honour thy father and thy mother, which is the first commandment with a promise:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               That it may be well with thee, and thou mayest be long lived upon earth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               And you, fathers, provoke not your children to anger; but bring them up in the discipline and correction of the Lord.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               Servants, be obedient to them that are your lords according to the flesh, with fear and trembling, in the simplicity of your heart, as to Christ:
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               Not serving to the eye, as it were pleasing men, but, as the servants of Christ doing the will of God from the heart,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               With a good will serving, as to the Lord, and not to men.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               Knowing that whatsoever good thing any man shall do, the same shall he receive from the Lord, whether he be bond, or free.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               And you, masters, do the same things to them, forbearing threatenings, knowing that the Lord both of them and you is in heaven; and there is no respect of persons with him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               Finally, brethren, be strengthened in the Lord, and in the might of his power.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               Put you on the armour of God, that you may be able to stand against the deceits of the devil.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               For our wrestling is not against flesh and blood; but against principalities and powers, against the rulers of the world of this darkness, against the spirits of wickedness
               <u>
                in the high places
               </u>
               .
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               Therefore take unto you the armour of God, that you may be able to resist in the evil day, and to stand in all things perfect.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               Stand therefore, having your loins girt about with truth, and having on the breastplate of justice,
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               And your feet shod with the preparation of the gospel of peace:
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [12]
               <u>
                "In the high places"
               </u>
               : or heavenly places. That is to say, in the air, the lowest of the celestial regions; in which God permits these wicked spirits or fallen angels to wander.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               In all things taking the shield of faith, wherewith you may be able to extinguish all the fiery darts of the most wicked one.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               And take unto you the helmet of salvation, and the sword of the Spirit (which is the word of God).
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               By all prayer and supplication praying at all times in the spirit; and in the same watching with all instance and supplication for all the saints:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=19-#x">
                [19]
               </a>
               And for me, that speech may be given me, that I may open my mouth with confidence, to make known the mystery of the gospel.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=20-#x">
                [20]
               </a>
               For which I am an ambassador in a chain, so that therein I may be bold to speak according as I ought.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=21-#x">
                [21]
               </a>
               But that you also may know the things that concern me, and what I am doing, Tychicus, my dearest brother and faithful minister in the Lord, will make known to you all things:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=22-#x">
                [22]
               </a>
               Whom I have sent to you for this same purpose, that you may know the things concerning us, and that he may comfort your hearts.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=23-#x">
                [23]
               </a>
               Peace be to the brethren and charity with faith, from God the Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=56&amp;ch=6&amp;l=24-#x">
                [24]
               </a>
               Grace be with all them that love our Lord Jesus Christ
               <u>
                in incorruption
               </u>
               . Amen.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [24]
               <u>
                "In incorruption"
               </u>
               : That is, with a pure and perfect love.
              </p>
              <p>
              </p>

             <td class="textarea" valign="top">
              <p class="desc">
               The vision of the woman clothed with the sun and of the great dragon her persecutor.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=1-#x">
                [1]
               </a>
               And a great sign appeared in heaven:
               <u>
                A woman
               </u>
               clothed with the sun, and the moon under her feet, and on her head a crown of twelve stars:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=2-#x">
                [2]
               </a>
               And being with child, she cried travailing in birth, and was in pain to be delivered.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=3-#x">
                [3]
               </a>
               And there was seen another sign in heaven: and behold a great red dragon, having seven heads, and ten horns: and on his head seven diadems:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=4-#x">
                [4]
               </a>
               And his tail drew the third part of the stars of heaven, and cast them to the earth: and the dragon stood before the woman who was ready to be delivered; that, when she should be delivered, he might devour her son.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=5-#x">
                [5]
               </a>
               And she brought forth a man child, who was to rule all nations with an iron rod: and her son was taken up to God, and to his throne.
              </p>
              <p class="note">
               [1]
               <u>
                "A woman"
               </u>
               : The church of God. It may also, by allusion, be applied to our blessed Lady. The church is clothed with the sun, that is, with Christ: she hath the moon, that is, the changeable things of the world, under her feet: and the twelve stars with which she is crowned, are the twelve apostles: she is in labour and pain, whilst she brings forth her children, and Christ in them, in the midst of afflictions and persecutions.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=6-#x">
                [6]
               </a>
               And the woman fled into the wilderness, where she had a place prepared by God, that there they should feed her a thousand two hundred sixty days.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=7-#x">
                [7]
               </a>
               And there was a great battle in heaven, Michael and his angels fought with the dragon, and the dragon fought and his angels:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=8-#x">
                [8]
               </a>
               And they prevailed not, neither was their place found any more in heaven.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=9-#x">
                [9]
               </a>
               And that great dragon was cast out, that old serpent, who is called the devil and Satan, who seduceth the whole world; and he was cast unto the earth, and his angels were thrown down with him.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=10-#x">
                [10]
               </a>
               And I heard a loud voice in heaven, saying: Now is come salvation, and strength, and the kingdom of our God, and the power of his Christ: because the accuser of our brethren is cast forth, who accused them before our God day and night.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=11-#x">
                [11]
               </a>
               And they overcame him by the blood of the Lamb, and by the word of the testimony, and they loved not their lives unto death.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=12-#x">
                [12]
               </a>
               Therefore rejoice, O heavens, and you that dwell therein. Woe to the earth, and to the sea, because the devil is come down unto you, having great wrath, knowing that he hath but a short time.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=13-#x">
                [13]
               </a>
               And when the dragon saw that he was cast unto the earth, he persecuted the woman, who brought forth the man child:
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=14-#x">
                [14]
               </a>
               And there were given to the woman two wings of a great eagle, that she might fly into the desert unto her place, where she is nourished for a time and times, and half a time, from the face of the serpent.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=15-#x">
                [15]
               </a>
               And the serpent cast out of his mouth after the woman, water as it were a river; that he might cause her to be carried away by the river.
              </p>
              <p>
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=16-#x">
                [16]
               </a>
               And the earth helped the woman, and the earth opened her mouth, and swallowed up the river, which the dragon cast out of his mouth.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=17-#x">
                [17]
               </a>
               And the dragon was angry against the woman: and went to make war with the rest of her seed, who keep the commandments of God, and have the testimony of Jesus Christ.
               <a href="/x/d?b=drb&amp;bk=73&amp;ch=12&amp;l=18-#x">
                [18]
               </a>
               And he stood upon the sand of the sea.
              </p>
